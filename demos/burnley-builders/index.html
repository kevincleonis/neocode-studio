What We Do

Full-Service Building Contractors

From foundations to finishing touches, we handle every stage of your build with the same care and attention to detail.

House Extensions

Single and double storey extensions that blend seamlessly with your existing property. We handle planning, design, and the full build from start to finish.

New House Builds

Complete new-build construction from the ground up. Our team manages every aspect of your project, keeping you informed at every milestone.

Barn Conversions

Specialist barn conversion work that preserves character while creating stunning modern living spaces. Experienced with listed buildings and heritage properties.

Brickwork & Pointing

Expert brickwork for new builds, repairs, and repointing. We match existing mortar and bricks for a flawless finish that stands the test of time.

Roofing & Loft Conversions

Complete roofing services including new roofs, repairs, and full loft conversions. Transform unused attic space into valuable living areas.

Kitchens & Bathrooms

Full kitchen and bathroom installations including plumbing, tiling, joinery, and fitting. We work with your chosen designs or help you plan from scratch.

Plastering & Rendering

Internal plastering and external rendering to protect and beautify your property. Smooth or textured finishes to match your requirements.

Driveways & Groundwork

Block paving, tarmac, and flagstone driveways plus drainage solutions. We handle all groundwork, foundations, and landscaping preparation.

Renovations & Repairs

General building maintenance, condensation repairs, structural work, and property renovations. No job is too small for our experienced team.

About Us

A Family Building Business You Can Trust

A&B Barker Ltd was founded in 2014 by the Barker family right here in Padiham, Burnley. What started as a small local outfit has grown into one of Lancashire's most respected building contractors, all without losing the personal touch that got us here.

With directors Alan and James Barker at the helm, every project gets hands-on oversight from day one. We don't just manage builds from behind a desk. We're on site, rolling up our sleeves, making sure every brick, beam, and finish meets the standard our name stands for.
