Aftercare

Look After Your Ink

Getting a great tattoo is only half the battle. Here's how you'll keep it looking fresh for years to come. We'll walk you through everything before you leave, but here's the essentials.

1

Keep It Wrapped

Leave the wrap on for 2-4 hours. Don't be tempted to peek early. Your skin needs time to start the healing process.

2

Wash Gently

Use lukewarm water and a fragrance-free soap. Pat it dry with a clean towel. Don't scrub, don't soak, don't submerge.

3

Moisturise Daily

Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser 2-3 times a day. We'll recommend the best one for your skin type.

4

Don't Pick or Scratch

It's going to itch. That's normal. But picking at scabs or peeling skin will pull the ink out. Let it heal naturally.

5

Avoid Sun & Water

No swimming, no sunbathing, no saunas for at least 2-3 weeks. After it's healed, always use SPF to keep colours sharp.
