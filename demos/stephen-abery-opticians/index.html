About Stephen

40 Years of Caring for Your Vision

Stephen Abery has been a full-time community optometrist since 1985. He founded this practice in 1991 with a simple belief: eye care should be personal, thorough, and completely free from sales pressure.

That approach earned him the role of GOC performance assessor and expert witness in primary care optometry. He's also spent 15 years as Chair of the Berkshire Local Optical Committee. But for his patients, what matters most is that he takes the time to listen, explain, and get it right.

Three generations of Reading families now trust Stephen Abery Opticians with their eye care. We'd love to welcome yours.
