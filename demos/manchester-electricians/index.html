What We Do

Electrical Services You Can Trust

Whether it's a simple socket installation or a complete commercial fit-out, our NICEIC approved electricians handle it all to the highest standard.

Domestic Electrical

Complete house rewires, additional sockets and lighting, new builds, extensions and loft conversions. All Part P compliant with certificates issued on completion.

Commercial Electrical

Office fit-outs, retail units, warehouse installations and maintenance contracts. We keep your business powered and compliant with all current regulations.

Industrial Services

Heavy-duty installations, three-phase power supplies, industrial lighting and machinery connections. Experienced with factory and warehouse environments.

LED Lighting

Energy-saving LED conversions and installations for homes and businesses. Reduce your electricity bills while improving the look and feel of your space.

Testing & Inspection

EICR reports, periodic testing, emergency lighting certification and fire alarm testing. Stay compliant with up-to-date electrical safety certificates.

Fuseboard Upgrades

Replace outdated fuse boxes with modern consumer units featuring RCD protection. Essential for home safety and often required for insurance compliance.
