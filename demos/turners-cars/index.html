Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Expertise

Turners Cars has been part of the Arnold community for over 15 years. What started as a small workshop has grown into one of the most trusted garages in Nottinghamshire.

Based at Unit 3, Eagle Close in the heart of Arnold, we've built our reputation one job at a time. From routine oil changes to complete engine rebuilds, we treat every car that comes through our doors like it's our own.

Our team is led by a City & Guilds qualified service manager with an NVQ Level 3 in Vehicle Mechanical and Electronic Systems. That means when you bring your car to us, you're getting properly trained professionals who know what they're doing.

We're proud to serve customers across Nottingham, Arnold, Hucknall, Mapperley, Carlton, Gedling and beyond. If you can drive it to us, we can fix it.
