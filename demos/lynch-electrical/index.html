What We Do

Electrical Services Done Right

From domestic rewires to commercial fit-outs, we've got you covered. Every job comes with a full guarantee and NICEIC certification.

Domestic Rewiring

Complete house rewires, consumer unit upgrades, and additional circuits. We'll bring your home's electrics up to current regulations with minimal disruption.

Commercial Electrical

Office fit-outs, retail installations, and industrial wiring. We work around your schedule to keep your business running without interruption.

Fire Alarm Systems

Design, installation, and maintenance of fire alarm systems for homes and commercial premises. Full compliance with current fire safety regulations.

LED Lighting

Energy-efficient LED upgrades for homes and businesses. Cut your energy bills and transform your space with modern, stylish lighting solutions.

Electrical Testing

EICR inspections, PAT testing, and periodic testing for landlords and businesses. Full certification and detailed reports included with every test.

Emergency Repairs

Power outage? Sparking socket? Don't wait around. We offer 24/7 emergency call-outs across Northern Ireland with fast response times.
