Our Services

Complete Locksmith
Solutions

From emergency lockouts to full security upgrades, we cover every lock-related need for homes and businesses.

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your home, car, or business? We provide rapid, non-destructive entry 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. No damage to your door.

Lock Changes

Moved into a new property or need to replace old locks? We supply and fit high-security locks from trusted brands at competitive prices.

Lock Repair

Stiff, jammed, or faulty locks repaired quickly and affordably. We fix all types of locks including mortice, cylinder, and multi-point systems.

Key Cutting

Precision key cutting for all lock types. Spare keys, replacement keys, and restricted key systems available on-site or in our workshop.

UPVC Lock Repair

Specialists in UPVC door and window lock mechanisms. We repair and replace gearboxes, handles, hinges, and multi-point locking systems.

Security Upgrades

Upgrade your property's security with British Standard locks, anti-snap cylinders, smart locks, and comprehensive security assessments.
