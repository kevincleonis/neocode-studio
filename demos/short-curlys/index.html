Our Story

More Than a Salon.
A Grimsby Institution.

When Tracy opened Short & Curlys on Cromwell Road in 1990, she had one simple promise: every client walks out feeling their absolute best. Over 35 years later, that promise hasn't changed — but the salon certainly has.

What looks unassuming from the outside opens into a modern, stylish interior that surprises first-time visitors. With complimentary Nespresso coffee, free WiFi, and four talented stylists, we've built a loyal following that spans generations of Grimsby families.

We invest in the best products and ongoing education — because your hair deserves nothing less.
