Our Story

A Family Tradition of Excellence

Since Tony Davies first picked up a brush back in 1982, we've been transforming homes and businesses across Wolverhampton and the West Midlands. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted partnership, but our values haven't changed one bit.

We believe in doing the job right. Every surface is properly prepared, every coat is carefully applied, and every project is treated like it's our own home. That's why our customers keep coming back, and why their neighbours keep calling us.

Based at Unit 12 on Landport Road, we're a proper local firm. We know the area, we know the houses, and we know what works in the West Midlands climate.

Guild of Master Craftsmen

Proudly accredited, reflecting our unwavering commitment to the highest standards of workmanship and professionalism.

What We Do

Our Services

From a single room refresh to complete commercial redecorations, we handle it all with the same care and attention to detail.

Interior Painting

Expert interior painting for every room in your home. We handle all prep work, from filling cracks to sanding surfaces, giving you a flawless finish every time.

Exterior Painting

Weather-resistant exterior painting that protects and beautifies your property. We use premium paints built to withstand the British weather for years to come.

Wallpapering

Precision wallpapering with perfect pattern matching. Whether it's feature walls or full rooms, we'll hang it beautifully with clean lines and no bubbles.

Commercial Painting

Minimal disruption, maximum impact. We work around your schedule to give offices, shops, and commercial properties a professional finish on time and on budget.

Restoration Work

Specialist restoration for period properties and heritage buildings. We understand traditional techniques and use appropriate materials to preserve character and charm.

Colour Consultation

Not sure what colour to go with? Our experienced team will help you choose the perfect palette for your space, taking into account lighting, furniture, and personal taste.

Our Journey

Over Four Decades of Craftsmanship

From humble beginnings to one of Wolverhampton's most trusted painting firms.

1982

Where It All Began

Tony Davies started his painting and decorating business in Wolverhampton with nothing but skill, determination, and a strong work ethic passed down from his father.

1995

Growing the Team

The business expanded into a partnership, bringing on experienced craftsmen to meet growing demand from both residential and commercial clients across the West Midlands.

2008

Guild of Master Craftsmen

Tony Davies & Partner LLP earned accreditation from the prestigious Guild of Master Craftsmen, recognising our consistently high standards of work.

2024

40+ Years and Still Going Strong

With over 10,000 projects completed, we continue to serve Wolverhampton and beyond. The same family values, the same dedication to quality, the same trust.
