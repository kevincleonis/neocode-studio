Our Story

A Brighton Institution on Eldred Avenue

Westdene Butchers has been the heart of the Westdene community since 2006. We're not just a butcher's shop — we're your neighbours, your trusted source for quality meat, and a proud champion of Sussex farming.

Every cut that crosses our counter comes from farms we know personally. Our Sussex beef and lamb graze on the South Downs, and our commitment to provenance and quality means you always know exactly where your meat comes from.

Locally Sourced

Beef and lamb from Sussex farms on the South Downs.

Expert Butchery

Traditional craft and precision in every cut we make.

African Heritage

Unique biltong and South African flavours you won't find elsewhere.

Community First

Proudly serving Westdene, Patcham, and all of Brighton.
