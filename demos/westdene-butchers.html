Our Story

A Brighton Institution
Built on Trust

When Trevor Morton took over the shop at 34 Eldred Avenue, the previous butcher had gone trade-only and was barely open one day a week. Trevor saw an opportunity to bring proper, community-focused butchery back to Westdene.

Today, Westdene Butchers is one of Brighton's most loved local shops. Trevor sources his beef and lamb directly from South Downs farmers, cutting down on food miles and making sure every penny supports local agriculture. The team here doesn't just sell meat - they'll talk you through the best cuts, share cooking tips, and make sure you leave with exactly what you need.

As the first "Barn Buddy" supporter of Friends of Westdene Green, this isn't just a butcher shop. It's a cornerstone of the community.

What We Offer

Quality You Can Taste

Every cut is hand-selected, properly hung for tenderness and flavour, and priced to beat the supermarkets. That's not a marketing line - it's a promise.

South Downs

Beef & Lamb

Sourced from South Downs farmers and hung to maturity. Ribeyes, sirloins, rumps, racks of lamb - all cut fresh daily and always cheaper than the supermarket.

Made to Order

Sausages & Burgers

All made fresh in-house from our own recipes. From classic Cumberland to South African boerewors packed with coriander and nutmeg. Just tell us what you fancy.

UK's Best

Biltong & Boerewors

Winner of Best Beef Biltong in the UK. Our South African specialties are legendary in Brighton - dried and cured in-house using traditional methods. Once you try it, you won't go anywhere else.

Free Range Poultry

Free-range chickens, ducks, and seasonal game birds. Properly reared, full of flavour, and sourced from trusted local farms across Sussex.

Deli & Cooked Meats

Home-cooked ham, pork pies, and a rotating selection of ready-to-eat favourites. Perfect for a quick lunch or adding something special to your cheese board.

Seasonal Specials

Christmas turkeys, Easter lamb, BBQ season boxes and more. We take advance orders so you never miss out on the perfect centrepiece for your table.

Why Choose Us

Skip the Supermarket.
Support Your Community.

🌱

Farm to Counter

We know every farmer by name. Our beef and lamb travels from the South Downs to our counter - not across continents. Fewer food miles, fresher meat, better flavour.

💪

Properly Hung & Aged

Unlike vacuum-packed supermarket cuts, our meat is hung to maturity. This old-school technique produces real tenderness and depth of flavour that you simply can't get elsewhere.

💰

Better Value Than You'd Think

Trevor puts it simply: "Pretty much all the meat here is less expensive than in supermarkets." Better quality, lower prices, zero food waste. Win-win-win.

🤝

Real Expertise, Real People

Don't know your bavette from your brisket? No problem. The team will talk you through cuts, cooking methods, and portion sizes. It's personal service you won't find at Tesco.
