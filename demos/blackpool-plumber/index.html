What I Do

Professional Plumbing Services

Whatever your plumbing problem, I've got the tools, the skills, and the experience to sort it out. Here's what I can help with.

🚪

Emergency Repairs

Burst pipe? Flooding? Don't panic. I offer fast emergency callouts across Blackpool and the Fylde Coast. I'll be there when you need me most.

🛀

Bathroom Installations

Full bathroom design and fitting, from budget-friendly refreshes to high-end walk-in showers and freestanding baths. All pipework, tiling, and finishing.

🔥

Boiler Repairs & Servicing

Annual boiler servicing, breakdown repairs, and system upgrades. I work with all major brands to keep your heating running efficiently.

🚰

Leak Detection & Repair

Hidden leaks can cause serious damage. I use modern detection methods to pinpoint and fix leaks before they become a bigger problem.

🏠

Central Heating

Radiator installations, power flushing, thermostat upgrades, and full central heating system installations. Stay warm this winter.

📦

General Plumbing

Taps, toilets, stopcocks, waste disposals, dishwasher and washing machine connections. No job is too small, and every job gets my full attention.
