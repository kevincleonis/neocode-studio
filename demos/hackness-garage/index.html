Our Story

Three generations,
one promise

Hackness Garage has been part of the village since 1960. What started as a small workshop has grown into a trusted name across Scarborough and the surrounding North Yorkshire countryside.

Run by the Hopper family for over 65 years, we're not a faceless chain. When you bring your car to us, you're dealing with time-served mechanics who genuinely care about getting the job done right. We work on everything from vintage classics to the latest models.

What We Do

Everything your car needs,
under one roof

From your annual MOT to a full engine rebuild, we've got the skills, the tools, and the experience to sort it.

📋

MOT Testing

Class 4 MOT testing for vehicles up to 7.5 tonnes. Quick turnaround, honest assessments, and we'll always talk you through the results.

⚙️

Full Servicing

Interim and full services to manufacturer specs. We service both vintage and modern vehicles, keeping your car running smoothly year-round.

🔧

Repairs

From minor fixes to major mechanical work. Brakes, clutches, suspension, electrics, engine repairs. We'll diagnose the problem and give you a straight answer.

🛞

Tyres

Competitive range of quality tyres in stock. We'll fit them, balance them, and check your alignment. All brands and sizes for cars and light commercials.

💨

Exhausts

Full exhaust system repairs and replacements. If it's blowing, rattling, or hanging low, we'll sort it out quickly and at a fair price.

❄️

Seasonal Checks

Summer and winter prep to make sure your car's ready for whatever North Yorkshire throws at it. Battery, coolant, tyres, wipers, the lot.
