About Us

A Small Company That Delivers Big Results

Les Bezance Building Services is a small, professional building company providing reliable services for domestic and commercial clients across the Middlesbrough and Stockton-on-Tees areas.

With over 20 years in the industry, we've built our reputation on superior quality workmanship at affordable prices. The majority of our new work comes through existing customer recommendations, and that's something we're genuinely proud of.

We're not a faceless corporate outfit. When you hire us, you're working directly with experienced tradesmen who take real pride in every project.
