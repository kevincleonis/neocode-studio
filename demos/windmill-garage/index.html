What We Do

Our Services

From routine servicing to complex repairs, we handle it all under one roof with the skill and care your vehicle deserves.

📋

MOT Testing

Official MOT testing station. We'll check everything to DVSA standards and give you a clear breakdown of any advisories or failures.

🔧

Full Servicing

Interim and full services to keep your car running smoothly. Oil changes, filter replacements, fluid top-ups and full inspections included.

⚫

Brake Repairs

Pads, discs, callipers and fluid. We'll make sure your brakes are in top condition so you can stop with confidence every time.

⚙

Clutch Replacement

Slipping clutch or heavy pedal? Our team handles full clutch replacements, dual mass flywheels and concentric slave cylinders.

⏱

Timing Belt

Don't wait until it snaps. We replace timing belts and water pumps at manufacturer-recommended intervals to protect your engine.

💻

Diagnostics

Dashboard warning light on? Our diagnostic equipment reads fault codes from all major systems so we can pinpoint the problem fast.

⬤

Tyres

Supply and fitting of quality tyres at competitive prices. We also offer wheel balancing, tracking and puncture repairs.
