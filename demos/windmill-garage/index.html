What We Do

Complete Vehicle Care Under One Roof

From routine maintenance to complex repairs, our team handles it all with precision and care.

MOT Testing

Approved DVSA testing station. Book your annual MOT with us and get a thorough, fair assessment. Free retest if needed within 10 working days.

Full Servicing

Interim and full service options tailored to your mileage and manufacturer requirements. We use quality parts and follow proper procedures.

Brakes & Clutch

Brake pads, discs, callipers, and complete clutch replacements. Your safety is everything. We won't cut corners on the parts that matter most.

Diagnostics

Engine warning light on? Our diagnostic equipment reads fault codes from all major systems. We'll find the problem and explain it in plain English.

Tyres & Alignment

New tyres from leading brands at competitive prices. Wheel balancing and tracking to keep your ride smooth and your tyres wearing evenly.

Exhausts & Emissions

Full exhaust system repairs, catalytic converter work, and emissions testing. Keep your vehicle running clean and sounding right.

General Repairs

Suspension, steering, electrical faults, timing belts, head gaskets, and everything in between. If it's broken, we can fix it.
