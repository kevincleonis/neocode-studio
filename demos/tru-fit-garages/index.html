About Us

Belfast's Most Trusted
Service Centre

Located at 442 Donegall Road, just off the M1 roundabout near the Royal Victoria Hospital, Tru-Fit Service Centre has been keeping Belfast drivers on the road for over three decades.

As Northern Ireland's only approved supplier of premium GT Exhaust Systems, we specialise in the supply and fitting of aluminised steel exhaust systems for most makes and models. But we're much more than exhausts — our skilled team handles everything from servicing and brakes to diagnostics and tyres.

  • Only approved GT Exhaust supplier in NI
  • Locally owned & managed for 30+ years
  • All makes and models welcome
  • Convenient location off the M1
