What We Do

Complete Electrical Services for
Your Home & Business

From full house rewires to EV charger installations, every job is completed to NICEIC standards with full certification.

Full & Partial Rewires

Complete house rewiring and partial upgrades to bring your property up to current BS7671 regulations. Minimal disruption, all work certified.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Modern RCD-protected fuse board replacements. The most important safety upgrade for any home with an outdated electrical panel.

EV Charger Installation

Home electric vehicle charging points for all major brands. Professional installation with dedicated circuit and full certification.

EICR Testing

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for landlords, homeowners, and insurance. Legal requirement for rental properties.

CCTV & Alarm Systems

Home security camera installation and intruder alarm systems. Protect your property with professionally installed and wired security.

Lighting & Smart Home

LED downlights, feature lighting, garden lighting, and smart home automation. Transform your spaces with expert lighting design.

PAT Testing

Portable Appliance Testing for businesses and landlords. Qualified PAT testers. Certificates and labels provided for all appliances.

Fault Finding & Repairs

Tripping circuits, flickering lights, power outages. Expert fault diagnosis and safe repair. Emergency callouts available.

Kitchen & Bathroom Electrics

Specialist wiring for kitchens, bathrooms, and wet rooms. Sockets, extractors, heated towel rails, and appliance connections.

Who We Are

A Family Business Built on Trust & Quality
