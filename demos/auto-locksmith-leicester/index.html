Auto Locksmith Services

Complete mobile locksmith solutions for all vehicles

Emergency Car Lockouts

Locked your keys in the car? Our mobile locksmiths reach you within 30 minutes and gain entry without damage.

Car Key Replacement

Lost all your car keys? We cut and programme new keys on-site for all makes including BMW, Audi, VW, Ford, and more.

Transponder Programming

Transponder and remote key programming. We code new keys to your vehicle's immobiliser system using dealer-level equipment.

Ignition Repair

Broken or jammed ignition? We repair and replace ignition barrels and switches on-site, getting you back on the road.

Key Fob Repair

Broken key fob? Buttons not working? We repair, replace casings and re-programme remote fobs for all vehicles.

Van & Commercial
