About Us

Edinburgh's Friendly
Moving Service

Based at 9 Morvenside, Edinburgh, we're a local man with a van service built on reliability, fair pricing, and genuine care for your belongings. Whether you're moving house, shifting furniture, or clearing a property, we treat every job with the same attention to detail.

We cover all Edinburgh postcodes and regularly travel across the Lothians and beyond. Our large van handles everything from single items to full house moves, and we're available 7 days a week to fit around your schedule.
