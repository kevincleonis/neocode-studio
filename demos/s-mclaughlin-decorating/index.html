What We Do

Our Professional Services

From residential refreshes to commercial fit-outs, we deliver beautiful results across every project.

Interior Painting

Refresh every room with clean lines, even coverage, and premium paints. Walls, ceilings, woodwork, and trim expertly finished.

Exterior Painting

Weather-resistant finishes that protect and transform. Fascias, soffits, walls, and fences treated to withstand Belfast conditions.

Wallpapering

Precision wallpaper hanging with seamless pattern matching. Feature walls, full rooms, and specialist papers handled with care.

Plastering

Smooth, level plaster finishes for walls and ceilings. Skim coats, patch repairs, and complete re-plasters to the highest standard.

Commercial Painting

Office fit-outs, retail units, restaurants, and commercial properties. Minimal disruption, efficient turnaround, professional results.

Feature Walls

Statement accent walls that add character to any room. Bold colours, designer wallpapers, and creative finishes to suit your style.
