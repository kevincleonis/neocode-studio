Norwich's Gold Standard in Taxi Service

We're not just another taxi firm. Gold Star Taxis has been serving Norwich and the wider Norfolk region for over 15 years, and we've built our reputation on reliability, professionalism, and treating every passenger like a VIP.

All our drivers are fully licensed, DBS checked, and trained to deliver a first-class experience. From your morning commute to a late-night airport run, you'll always get there safely and on time.
