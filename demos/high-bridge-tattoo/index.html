Tattoo Aftercare

Your tattoo's only as good as how you look after it. Follow these steps and it'll heal perfectly.

⚗

First 24 Hours

  • Keep the wrap on for 2-4 hours
  • Wash gently with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap
  • Pat dry with a clean paper towel
  • Apply a thin layer of aftercare balm
  • Don't re-wrap unless your artist says to
☃

Week 1-2

  • Wash 2-3 times daily with clean hands
  • Apply aftercare cream sparingly
  • Don't pick, scratch, or peel the flaking skin
  • Avoid submerging in water (baths, pools, sea)
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing over the area
☀

Long Term Care

  • Always apply SPF 30+ over healed tattoos
  • Keep your skin moisturised daily
  • Avoid tanning beds and prolonged sun exposure
  • Touch-ups are free within the first year
  • Contact us if you notice anything unusual during healing
