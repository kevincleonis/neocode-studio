Aftercare Guide

Your new tattoo is an open wound and needs proper care to heal beautifully. We provide full written aftercare instructions at your appointment, but here are the basics:

  • Keep the wrap on for 2–4 hours after your session
  • Wash gently with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap
  • Apply a thin layer of recommended aftercare cream
  • Avoid direct sunlight, swimming, and soaking for 2 weeks
  • Don't pick or scratch — let it peel naturally
  • Contact us if you have any concerns during healing
