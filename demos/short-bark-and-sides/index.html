What We Do

Our Grooming Services

From a simple shampoo to the full pamper treatment, every dog gets individual care and attention.

Full Dog Grooming

Complete grooming service including bath, blow dry, brush out, haircut, nail trim and ear clean. Your dog leaves looking and smelling fantastic.

Hand Stripping

Expert hand stripping for wire and rough coated breeds. Maintains proper coat texture and colour. Refreshes the coat every 8-10 weeks.

Puppy Hygiene

Gentle introduction to grooming for puppies. We take extra care with young pups, building positive associations from their very first visit.

Peddymark Microchipping

Fully qualified to implant Peddymark microchips for all breeds. Registered on Petlog, the Kennel Club database, for complete peace of mind.

Meet the Owner

Born Into a Love of Animals

I grew up on a farm, surrounded by animals from day one. My father breeds and trains working labradors, and I've owned dogs all my life. Short Bark and Sides sprang into existence in 2010 from a love of everything canine.

I trained under Zoe Duffy and Joanne Angus at their UK award-winning grooming centre. I'm a perfectionist and I get real satisfaction from a great cut. I only take a few appointments per day so every dog gets my complete focus.

There's always a free cup of tea and a biscuit on hand if you want one.

CS
Collette Sykes Owner & Head Groomer
