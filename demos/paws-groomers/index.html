Our Services

Everything Your Dog Deserves

From a full pamper session to a quick nail trim, we offer a complete range of grooming services tailored to every breed and temperament.

Full Groom

The complete treatment: bath, blow-dry, brush, haircut, nail trim, ear clean and finishing touches. Your dog will leave looking and feeling fantastic.

Bath & Brush

A thorough bath with premium shampoo and conditioner, followed by a professional blow-dry and full brush-out. Perfect for maintaining a healthy coat.

Nail Clipping

Quick and careful nail trimming to keep your dog comfortable and prevent overgrown nails. A stress-free experience for even the most nervous pups.

De-matting

Gentle removal of tangles and mats using specialist tools and techniques. We work patiently to restore your dog's coat without discomfort or distress.

De-shedding

Specialist treatment to remove excess undercoat and reduce shedding. Ideal for double-coated breeds and keeping your home fur-free between visits.

Puppy Introductions

A calm, gentle first grooming experience designed to build confidence and positive associations. We take it at your puppy's pace to set them up for life.
