What We Do

Tattoo Styles

Every piece is custom designed. We don't do cookie-cutter flash unless you want it. Tell us your vision and we'll bring it to life on skin.

✎

Fine Line

Delicate, intricate linework that captures detail with surgical precision. Perfect for script, minimalist designs, and botanical pieces.

☯

Neo Traditional

Bold outlines meet vibrant colour palettes. A modern take on classic tattoo art with richer detail and dimensional shading.

♦

Blackwork

From geometric patterns to full black coverage, we specialise in bold, graphic pieces that make a statement.

✿

Realism

Portraits, nature, and photorealistic detail. We bring your references to life with depth, contrast, and tonal accuracy.

❖

Illustrative

Where tattoo meets illustration. Sketchy, painterly, or comic-inspired pieces with character and personality.

✧

Piercings

Professional body and ear piercings using top-quality jewellery. Nose, ears, nipple piercings and more, done safely.
