About Us

Barnsley's Friendly Local Cafe & Takeaway

Barnsley Takeaway & Cafe is your neighbourhood go-to for proper food done right. Whether you're grabbing a quick coffee and a sandwich on the way to work, or sitting down for a hearty hot meal, we serve everything fresh with a smile.

Located on Market Street in the heart of Barnsley, we're proud to offer generous portions, honest prices, and the kind of friendly service that keeps our regulars coming back day after day.
