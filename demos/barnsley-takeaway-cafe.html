Our Story

A Local Favourite
in Barnsley

We're a family-owned takeaway and cafe right in the heart of Barnsley, NSW. What started as a simple fish and chip shop has grown into the go-to spot for locals who want honest, well-cooked food without the wait or the price tag.

We're not trying to be fancy. We're just trying to make sure every fish is crispy, every burger is loaded, and every coffee hits the spot. That's the standard we hold ourselves to, day in, day out.
