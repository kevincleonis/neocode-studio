What We Do

Professional Locksmith Services

From emergency lockouts to full security upgrades, we've got Belfast covered.

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your home, office, or car? We'll have you back inside within 30 minutes. Available 24/7, 365 days a year. No damage entry where possible.

Lock Change & Repair

Moved into a new property? Need your locks upgraded? We supply and fit all major brands including Yale, Chubb, and ERA. British Standard locks available.

Learn more

UPVC & Door Locks

Specialists in UPVC door and window lock mechanisms. We repair and replace multipoint locking systems, handles, cylinders, and euro locks for all door types.

Safe Opening

Lost the combination or key to your safe? Our technicians can open most safes without damage. We also supply and install new safes for homes and businesses.

Security Upgrades
