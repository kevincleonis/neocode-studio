About Us

Glasgow's All-Trades Tiling & Bathroom Company

Tiler Glasgow has grown into a successful, thriving business providing an all-trades bathroom and tiling service throughout Glasgow, Lanarkshire and the West of Scotland.

What sets us apart is simple: you don't need to coordinate multiple tradespeople. Our team includes joiners, plumbers, plasterers, electricians and decorators, all working together to deliver your project on time and on budget.

We pride ourselves on exceptional attention to detail, competitive pricing and a friendly, professional approach from start to finish.
