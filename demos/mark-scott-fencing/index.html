Meet Mark Scott

With over 15 years in the fencing trade, Mark's built a reputation for honest work, quality materials, and fences that genuinely stand the test of time. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted local business that homeowners come back to again and again.

Our Fencing & Gate Services

From a simple fence repair to a full garden transformation, we've got you covered. Every job gets the same attention to detail, no matter the size.

Panel Fencing

Our most popular choice for residential boundaries. We supply and fit high-quality lap panel and overlap fencing that looks great and provides solid privacy for your garden.

Close Board Fencing

The strongest, most durable option for any boundary. Built on-site with individual featherboard slats, concrete posts, and gravel boards for a fence that'll last decades.
