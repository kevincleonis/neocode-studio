Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before getting inked.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Pricing depends on the size, detail, placement, and time required. Small pieces start from £60. We offer free consultations where we can provide an accurate quote for your design.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Absolutely! Walk-ins are always welcome, subject to artist availability. For larger or more complex pieces, we recommend booking in advance to guarantee your preferred artist and time slot.

What age do I need to be?

You must be 18 or over to get a tattoo. We require a valid photo ID (passport or driving licence) before any work begins. No exceptions.

How do I book a consultation?

You can call us, email us, or drop into the studio. Consultations are always free and there's no pressure. Bring any reference images or ideas and we'll work together to create something perfect.

What about aftercare?

We provide a detailed aftercare guide with every tattoo. Keep it clean, moisturised, and out of direct sunlight during healing. We're always available if you have any questions during recovery.
