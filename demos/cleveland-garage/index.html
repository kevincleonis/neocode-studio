MOT Testing

Straightforward MOTs, No Hidden Agenda

Your vehicle needs an MOT once it's 3 years old. We test cars, vans and minibuses to the standards required by law, and we'll explain everything clearly.

  • Free re-test within 2 weeks if you don't pass
  • Honest assessment of advisories and failures
  • No pressure to get repairs done here
  • Class 4, 5 and 7 vehicles tested
  • Competitive repairs if you choose to stay
  • Book by phone or walk in
💡
Pro tip: You can get your MOT done up to a month early without losing your renewal date. Don't leave it to the last minute.
