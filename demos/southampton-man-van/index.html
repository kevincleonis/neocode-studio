Free Resource

Your Moving Day Checklist

Moving can be stressful. Use our checklist to stay organised and make your moving day as smooth as possible.

1

4 Weeks Before

Start decluttering. Donate, sell, or bin items you no longer need. Less stuff = cheaper move.

2

2 Weeks Before

Start packing non-essentials. Label every box with room name and contents. Notify utility providers.

3

1 Week Before

Confirm your booking with us. Pack an essentials box (kettle, mugs, chargers, toiletries, snacks).

4

The Day Before

Finish packing. Defrost the freezer. Confirm parking arrangements at both addresses.

5

Moving Day

We arrive, load up, and transport everything safely. You just tell us where each box goes.

6

After the Move

Take meter readings. Unpack essentials first. Put the kettle on — you've earned it!
