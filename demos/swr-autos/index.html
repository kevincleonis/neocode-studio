What We Do

Our Services

Everything your car needs under one roof. From routine MOTs to complex diagnostics, we've got it covered.

MOT Testing

DVSA-approved MOT testing station. We'll check everything and give you honest results with no pressure to repair here.

Servicing

Interim and full servicing using quality parts. We follow manufacturer guidelines to keep your warranty intact.

Brakes

Pads, discs, fluid changes and full brake system inspections. Your safety is never something we compromise on.

Exhausts

Full exhaust system repairs and replacements. Catalytic converters, DPF cleaning and emissions work included.

Diagnostics

Advanced diagnostic equipment to read fault codes and pinpoint issues fast. Saves you time and money on repairs.

Tyres

New tyres supplied and fitted from leading brands. Balancing, alignment and pressure checks all available.

Air Conditioning

Full air con regas and leak detection. Don't wait for summer, get it sorted now and stay comfortable year round.
