Our Story

Blackpool's Hair Destination Since 1985

Topps Hair Fashion isn't just a salon. It's a place where you can relax, be yourself, and walk out feeling like a million pounds. We've been part of the Blackpool community for over three decades, and we still love what we do.

Our talented team stays on top of the latest trends and techniques, but we also know that the best style is one that makes you feel confident. Whether you're after a fresh cut, a bold new colour, or something special for a big occasion, we've got you covered.

What We Do

Our Services

✂

Precision Cuts

From classic bobs to modern textured layers, our stylists craft the perfect cut for your face shape and lifestyle. Every cut includes a wash and blowdry.

🎨

Colour & Highlights

Full head colour, balayage, foils, T-section highlights and more. We use top-quality products for vibrant, long-lasting results that won't damage your hair.

✨

Blow Dry & Styling

Need to look your best for a special night out or event? Our blowdry and styling services will have you turning heads wherever you go.

💍

Bridal & Occasions

Your big day deserves the perfect hair. We offer full bridal packages including trials, bridesmaids, and mother of the bride styling.

💡

Perms & Treatments

Whether you're after bouncy curls or a deep conditioning treatment, we've got the expertise to give your hair exactly what it needs.

🌟

Hair Extensions

Add length, volume, or both with our professional hair extension services. We offer consultations to find the best match for your natural hair.

💃

Gents Styling

It's not just for the ladies. We offer sharp, modern cuts for men too. From fades to classic trims, walk in looking good, walk out looking great.
