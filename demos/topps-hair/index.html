Welcome to Topps Hair Fashion

Located in the heart of Blackpool on Warley Road, Topps Hair Fashion has been helping locals look and feel amazing for years. We're not just a salon, we're part of the community.

Whether you're popping in for a quick trim, a full colour transformation, or getting ready for your big day, our friendly team is here to make sure you leave with a smile (and incredible hair, obviously).

What We Do

From classic cuts to bold colour, we've got every look covered. Here's what you can book in for.

✂

Ladies Cut & Style

Precision cuts tailored to your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Includes a wash and blow dry.

✄

Gents Cut

Sharp, clean cuts for the guys. Whether it's a classic look or something modern, we've got you sorted.

🎨

Full Colour

A complete colour change or root touch-up using professional products that keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

✨

Highlights & Lowlights

Add dimension and depth with foils or balayage. Our colourists will create the perfect blend for you.

💫

Perms

Bouncy curls or soft waves that last. We use gentle formulas to get beautiful texture without damage.

💐

Bridal Styling

Your big day deserves perfect hair. We offer trials and on-the-day styling to make sure you look stunning.

💨

Blow Dry

A gorgeous, salon-fresh blow dry for when you want to look great without the full cut. Perfect for events.
