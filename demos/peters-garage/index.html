About Peter's Garage

An Honest Garage You Can Actually Trust

Founded by Piotr in 2009 with a simple idea: give people a garage they don't have to worry about. No surprise charges, no unnecessary repairs, no jargon you can't understand.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience and a fully equipped workshop on Midland Road, we've built a reputation across Scunthorpe and North Lincolnshire for doing things properly. Our diagnostic equipment covers more car makes than any other independent garage in the area.

We specialise in diesel injection systems and VW Group vehicles (Audi, Seat, Skoda, VW), but we work on pretty much everything that rolls through the door.
