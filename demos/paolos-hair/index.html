Our Story

A Touch of Italian Flair

Paolo's Hair Design isn't just a salon. It's an experience rooted in Italian craftsmanship and a passion for making every client feel extraordinary.

Located in the heart of Blackpool on Clifton Street, we blend timeless technique with modern trends. Every cut, colour, and style is treated as a work of art.

From classic blowouts to bold transformations, our talented team brings warmth, expertise, and that signature Italian attention to detail to every appointment.
