Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Taste

Shapla Indian Restaurant has been a cornerstone of Armagh's dining scene for over two decades. Nestled on Lower English Street, we bring the vibrant flavours of the Indian subcontinent to your plate, using recipes passed down through generations.

Every dish is prepared from scratch using the freshest ingredients and hand-ground spices. Whether you're craving a classic chicken tikka masala, a fiery vindaloo, or one of our signature biryanis, you'll taste the difference that real passion makes.

Our warm, welcoming atmosphere and attentive service make every visit a special occasion. From intimate dinners to family celebrations, Shapla is where memories are made.

The Shapla Difference

🌶

Authentic Recipes

Every dish follows traditional family recipes, refined over generations to bring you the genuine taste of India.

🔥

Clay Oven Cooking

Our traditional tandoor oven reaches temperatures over 500 degrees, giving our bread and meats that signature char and smokiness.

🌿

Fresh Ingredients

We source locally where we can and import specialty spices directly from India. No shortcuts, no compromises.

🍴

Dietary Options
