Welcome to Shapla Indian Restaurant

Tucked away on Lower English Street in the heart of Armagh, Shapla has been bringing the authentic flavours of India to Northern Ireland for years. Every dish we serve is a celebration of tradition, spice, and soul.

Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and time-honoured recipes to create dishes that are bursting with flavour. Whether you're joining us for a quiet dinner, a family celebration, or a quick takeaway, we promise you an experience that keeps you coming back.

From our hand-ground spice blends to our tandoor-fired breads, everything at Shapla is crafted with care and passion.

Why Choose Shapla?

🌶

Fresh Ingredients

We source our ingredients locally wherever possible and grind our spices in-house daily. No shortcuts, no frozen meals. Just real, honest food.

📖

Family Recipes

Our recipes have been passed down through generations. Each dish carries the warmth and wisdom of traditional Indian home cooking.

Made with Love
