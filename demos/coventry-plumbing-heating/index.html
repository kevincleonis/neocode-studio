What We Do

Our Professional Services

From emergency boiler repairs to full bathroom installations, we deliver expert workmanship you can rely on.

Boiler Installation

Expert installation of leading boiler brands including Worcester Bosch, Vaillant and Baxi. Energy-efficient systems with manufacturer warranty.

Boiler Repair

Fast diagnosis and repair for all boiler makes and models. We carry common parts on our vans for same-day fixes wherever possible.

Central Heating

Full central heating system design, installation and maintenance. Zoned controls, smart thermostats and energy-saving upgrades.

Plumbing Repairs

Leaking taps, burst pipes, blocked drains and toilet repairs. Prompt response and professional solutions every time.

Bathroom Fitting

Complete bathroom design and installation from start to finish. Tiling, plumbing, electrics and luxury fixtures all handled.

Gas Safety & Power Flushing

Landlord gas safety certificates, annual boiler servicing and MagnaCleanse power flushing to restore heating system efficiency.
