Post-Session

Tattoo Aftercare

Your tattoo is only as good as how you look after it. Follow these steps and it'll heal beautifully.

01

Keep It Wrapped

Leave the wrap on for 2-4 hours after your session. This protects against bacteria and absorbs any excess ink or fluid.

02

Wash Gently

Use lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap. Pat dry with a clean paper towel. Don't rub, don't scrub, don't soak.

03

Moisturise Daily

Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser 2-3 times a day. We recommend Hustle Butter or plain coconut oil.

04

Don't Pick or Scratch

Your tattoo will peel and itch as it heals. That's normal. Leave it alone and let your skin do its thing.

05

Avoid Sun & Water

No swimming, no baths, no sunbathing for at least 2 weeks. Showers are fine. Once healed, always use SPF 50+.

06

Free Touch-Ups

If anything needs touching up after healing, we'll sort it for free within 3 months. Just drop us a message to book in.
