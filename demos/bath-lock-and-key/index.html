Our Services

Everything You Need, All Under One Roof

From emergency lockouts to full security upgrades, we handle it all. Here's what we can do for you.

Emergency Lockout

Locked out? Don't stress. We'll get you back in quickly with minimal fuss and no damage to your lock where possible. Available 24/7, every day of the year.

Lock Changes & Upgrades

Moving home? Had a break-in? We'll fit new, high-security locks to British Standard specifications. We supply and fit all major brands including Yale, ERA and Ultion.

UPVC & Multipoint Locks

We're specialists in UPVC door and window locks. Whether it's a stuck mechanism or a full multipoint lock replacement, we've got the experience to sort it out.

Safe Opening

Lost the combination or key to your safe? We can open most makes and models without causing damage, and we'll set you up with new access straight away.

Key Cutting

Need spare keys cut? We can cut most types of keys on-site, including cylinder keys, mortice keys and high-security keys. Fast turnaround, fair prices.

Security Surveys

Not sure if your property's secure enough? We'll carry out a full security survey and recommend practical improvements that won't break the bank. Free, no-obligation assessments.
