About Kevin

Dundee's Trusted Mobile Dog Groomer

Kevin has been grooming dogs across Dundee and the surrounding area for years, building a loyal following of happy customers and even happier pups. With a 98% recommendation rate, his gentle approach and professional results speak for themselves.

As a mobile groomer, Kevin comes directly to your door — meaning less stress for your dog and more convenience for you. No cages, no car rides, no anxiety. Just professional grooming in a familiar, comfortable environment.
