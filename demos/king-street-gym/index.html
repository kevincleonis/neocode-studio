World-Class Facilities

Everything you need under one roof. No excuses, no compromises.

Free Weights

Dumbbells up to 60kg, Olympic bars, squat racks, bench stations, and a deadlift platform. Everything you need to build serious strength.

Cardio Zone

Treadmills, rowers, bikes, ski ergs, and assault bikes. Burn fat, build endurance, and leave it all on the floor.

Group Classes

From HIIT and spin to yoga and pilates. 50+ classes per week, all included with your membership. No booking fees.

Personal Training

One-on-one sessions with certified coaches who'll build a programme around your goals. Nutrition guidance included.

Boxing Area

Heavy bags, speed bags, a full-size ring, and boxing-specific classes. Whether you're training for a fight or just blowing off steam.

Functional Zone

Battle ropes, sleds, plyo boxes, TRX, kettlebells, and turf track. Train like an athlete, move like one too.
