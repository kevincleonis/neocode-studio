About Us

Meet Emma

Beauty Attraction was founded by Emma Loosemore in 2013, right here in Scunthorpe. What started as a passion for beauty has grown into a trusted local salon with over 15 years of experience in the hair and beauty industry.

Emma prides herself on keeping up with the latest trends and techniques while making sure every client gets treated like family. Whether you're after a full set of lashes, a fresh set of nails, or a new hair colour, you'll always leave feeling your best.

We also offer training courses for aspiring beauty professionals, so if you've ever fancied a career in beauty, we'd love to help you get started.
