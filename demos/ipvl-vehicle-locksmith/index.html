What We Do

Professional Auto Locksmith Services

From emergency lockouts to key programming, we provide a complete range of vehicle locksmith services across Plymouth and Devon.

Car Key Cutting

Precision key cutting for all makes and models. We carry a comprehensive range of key blanks and can cut your new key on-site at your location.

Key Programming

Advanced transponder and smart key programming using the latest diagnostic equipment. We program keys for most vehicle manufacturers.

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your vehicle? We offer rapid roadside response across Plymouth and Devon. Available 24/7 for emergency call-outs with no hidden fees.

Lock Repairs

Expert diagnosis and repair of faulty vehicle locks, ignition barrels, and door mechanisms. We restore full functionality without costly replacements.

Remote & Fob Replacement

Supply and program replacement remote key fobs, flip keys, and proximity keys. We stock a wide range to cover most popular vehicle brands.

Van & Motorcycle Keys

Specialist key services for commercial vans and motorcycles. Whether you drive a Transit or ride a Honda, we have the equipment and expertise.
