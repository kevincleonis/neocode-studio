What We Do

Our Services

From routine MOTs to specialist diagnostics, we offer a complete range of services to keep your vehicle running at its best.

📋

MOT Testing

DVSA-approved MOT testing for Class 4 vehicles. We'll give you a clear, honest assessment with no pressure to carry out unnecessary work.

🔧

Full Servicing

Interim and full services following manufacturer guidelines. Oil change, filter replacements, fluid top-ups and thorough multi-point inspections.

⚙

Brakes

Brake pad and disc replacement, fluid changes, and full brake system inspections. Your safety is always our top priority.

🛠

Clutch Repair

Complete clutch replacement and repairs. We handle dual-mass flywheel issues, clutch slippage, and hydraulic system faults.

💻

Diagnostics

Advanced engine diagnostics with professional-grade equipment. We pinpoint the exact issue before recommending any repairs.

⬤

Tyres

New tyre fitting, puncture repairs, wheel balancing and alignment. We stock a wide range of budget and premium tyres from top brands.

❄

Air Conditioning

Full air con service including regas, leak detection and system repairs. Stay cool in summer and clear in winter.
