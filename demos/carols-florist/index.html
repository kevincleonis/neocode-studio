Our Flowers

Hand-Tied Bouquets

Stunning hand-tied bouquets crafted fresh daily for birthdays, anniversaries, thank-yous, and those "just because" moments. Each arrangement is as unique as the person receiving it.

Wedding & Event Flowers

Make your special day unforgettable with bespoke wedding flowers. From bridal bouquets to venue arrangements, we create floral designs that bring your vision to life.

Funeral Tributes

Compassionate and personal funeral flower arrangements, including wreaths, hearts, posies, coffin sprays, and bespoke designs. Every tribute is crafted with sensitivity and care.

Gift Arrangements

Click & Collect

UK & Worldwide Delivery

Our Story

Over 57 Years of Floral Artistry

Carol's Florist has been a beloved part of the Manchester community for over five decades. As an independent, family-run florist, we take pride in providing daily fresh flowers, helpful and friendly advice, and exceptional customer care that you simply won't find at a chain.

Located at 508 Liverpool Road, Peel Green, Eccles, our shop is a destination for anyone who appreciates beautifully crafted floral arrangements made with genuine passion and artistry.
