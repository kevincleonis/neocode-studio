Our Story

A Family Tradition of Floral Artistry

Carol's Florist has been a beloved part of the Manchester community for over five decades. As an independent, family-run florist, we take pride in providing daily fresh flowers, genuine warmth, and the kind of personalised service you simply won't find at a chain.

From our shop at 508 Liverpool Road, Peel Green, Eccles, we've been creating unforgettable arrangements for weddings, funerals, anniversaries, and every moment that matters in between.

  • Daily fresh flowers and seasonal foliage
  • Independent, family-run since 1967
  • Interflora member — worldwide delivery
  • Compassionate funeral tribute specialists
  • Personalised advice for every occasion
