Our Story

A Family Tradition of Beautiful Flowers

Since 1967, Carol's Florist has been a beloved part of the Eccles community. What started as a small flower shop on Liverpool Road has grown into one of Manchester's most trusted family florists, now proudly run by the next generation.

We're not just about selling flowers. We're about helping you say what words sometimes can't. Whether it's celebrating a new arrival, marking an anniversary, or honouring someone special, every arrangement we create is made with genuine care and attention to detail.

Fresh flowers and foliage arrive at our shop every single day, so you'll always get the best quality blooms at honest prices.
