Meet Paul — Your Dog's New Best Friend

With over 15 years of professional grooming experience, Paul has dedicated his career to making every dog feel safe, comfortable, and beautiful. His calm and patient approach means even the most nervous pups leave with tails wagging.

Paul's Pooch Boutique was born from a simple belief: every dog deserves to be treated with love and care. We use only premium, dog-friendly products and maintain a calm, stress-free salon environment.

Whether your furry friend is a regular visitor or coming in for the very first time, Paul and the team will make sure they have an experience they'll love coming back to.
