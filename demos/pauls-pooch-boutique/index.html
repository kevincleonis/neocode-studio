Meet Paul

A Groomer Who Genuinely Loves What He Does

Paul's been grooming dogs for over a decade, and honestly, it's not just a job for him. It's his calling. Every dog that walks through the door gets treated like Paul's own, with patience, care, and plenty of treats along the way.

What We Offer

Services Tailored to Every Pooch

From a quick nail trim to the full pampering package, we've got everything your furry friend needs to look and feel amazing.

✂️

Full Groom

The complete package: bath, dry, brush, haircut, nail trim, ear clean, and a spritz of finishing cologne. Your dog won't want to leave.

🐾

Puppy Introduction

First groom? We'll take it slow and easy. This gentle intro session helps your puppy build confidence and associate grooming with positive vibes.

🛁

Bath & Tidy

A thorough wash, blow-dry, and tidy-up around the face, paws, and sanitary areas. Perfect for in-between full grooms.

💅

Nail Clipping

Quick, safe, and stress-free nail trimming. We'll get those nails to a comfortable length so your dog can walk and play without any trouble.

🪮

De-Shedding Treatment

Say goodbye to tumbleweeds of fur. Our de-shedding treatment removes loose undercoat and leaves your dog's coat smooth and healthy.

🦷

Teeth Cleaning

Fresh breath and healthy gums. We'll gently clean your dog's teeth to help prevent plaque build-up and keep that smile sparkling.
