Aftercare Guide

Look After Your New Ink

Proper aftercare is just as important as the tattoo itself. Follow these steps to keep your piece looking sharp.

First 2 Hours

Keep the wrap on for 2-3 hours after your session. Don't peek early. When you remove it, wash gently with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap.

Days 1-3

Wash 2-3 times a day and apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser. Don't soak it, don't scratch it, and keep it out of direct sunlight.

Week 1-2

Your tattoo will start to peel. That's totally normal. Don't pick at it. Keep moisturising and let it heal naturally. Avoid pools, baths and saunas.

Long Term

Always use SPF on your tattoo when you're in the sun. UV rays will fade your ink over time. Keep it moisturised and it'll stay vibrant for years.
