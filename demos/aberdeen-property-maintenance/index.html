About APM24-7

Your Local Property Maintenance Partner

Aberdeen Property Maintenance has been keeping homes and businesses across the Granite City in top condition for over 15 years. We're a team of skilled tradespeople who take real pride in every job, whether it's fixing a leaky tap or managing a full renovation.

We don't believe in cutting corners. Every project gets the same level of care and attention, because your property deserves nothing less. And with our 24/7 emergency service, you'll never be left waiting when something goes wrong.

What We Do

Professional Property Services

We cover every aspect of property maintenance under one roof. No need to call six different tradespeople when you can call us.

Plumbing

Burst pipes, blocked drains, boiler repairs, bathroom installations and everything in between. We'll sort it quickly and properly.

Electrical

Rewiring, fuse boards, lighting, socket installations and fault finding. All work carried out to the highest safety standards.

Joinery

Doors, windows, kitchens, decking, fencing and bespoke carpentry. We build things that last and look great doing it.

Painting & Decorating

Interior and exterior painting, wallpapering, plastering and everything you need to freshen up your space. Clean lines, every time.

Roofing

Slate repairs, flat roofing, guttering, fascias and soffits. We'll keep the Scottish weather where it belongs: outside.

Garden Maintenance

Lawn care, hedge trimming, tree surgery, patio laying and seasonal clean-ups. We'll keep your outdoor space looking its best all year round.
