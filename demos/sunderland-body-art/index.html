Look After Your Art

Aftercare Advice

Getting the tattoo or piercing is just the beginning. Proper aftercare is what keeps it looking sharp for years. Here's what you need to know.

Tattoo Aftercare

  • Keep the wrap on for 2-4 hours after your session
  • Wash gently with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap
  • Apply a thin layer of aftercare balm 2-3 times daily
  • Don't pick, scratch, or peel any scabbing
  • Avoid swimming, saunas, and direct sunlight for 2-3 weeks
  • Wear loose clothing over the tattooed area
  • Once healed, always apply SPF to protect your ink

Piercing Aftercare

  • Clean twice daily with saline solution for the first 6-8 weeks
  • Don't touch your piercing with unwashed hands
  • Avoid sleeping directly on a new piercing
  • Don't twist, turn, or play with the jewellery
  • Avoid swimming pools and hot tubs until fully healed
  • Don't change jewellery until the piercing is completely healed
  • Contact us immediately if you notice unusual swelling or discharge
