Our Services
Everything Your Chimney Needs

From routine sweeps to safety inspections, we keep your home safe and your fireplace working beautifully.

🧹

Chimney Sweeping

Thorough power sweeping using the latest rotary equipment. We remove soot, creosote and blockages to keep your chimney drawing safely.

🔍

Safety Inspections

Comprehensive CCTV chimney inspections to identify cracks, deterioration, bird nests and potential hazards before they become dangerous.

🐦

Bird Guard & Cowl Fitting

Professional installation of chimney cowls, bird guards and rain caps to prevent blockages and protect your flue from the elements.

🏠

Stove Installation

Expert advice and installation of wood burning and multi-fuel stoves, fully compliant with Building Regulations and HETAS standards.

📋

Certificates & Insurance

Every sweep includes an official certificate for your insurance records. Essential documentation for home insurance compliance.

🔧

Chimney Repairs

Repointing, relining and flue repairs to extend the life of your chimney. We fix problems before they become costly emergencies.
