Why NG Autos

Garage Quality. Doorstep Convenience.

We started NG Autos because we were sick of seeing people get overcharged at garages for work they didn't even need. We keep things simple: fair prices, honest advice, and work you can trust.

  • No Call-Out Fee

    We don't charge you just for showing up. The quote is for the job itself.

  • Genuine Parts Only

    We use OEM and quality aftermarket parts. No cheap knock-offs that fail after six months.

  • No Upselling

    We'll never tell you something needs fixing if it doesn't. If it's fine, we'll say so.

  • Fully Insured & DBS Checked

    Your car and your property are safe. We're professionals, not cowboys.
