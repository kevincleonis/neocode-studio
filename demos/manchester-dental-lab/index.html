Our Story

A Family Business Built on Trust

Founded in 1989 by Chris Humphreys, Manchester Dental Lab has grown from a small workshop into one of the region's most respected dental laboratories. With over 30 years of hands-on experience as a Dental Technician, Chris became a qualified Clinical Dental Technician (CDT) in 2014, allowing him to work directly with patients.

Today, the family tradition continues with his son Ryan Humphreys, who qualified as a CDT in 2025. Together, they lead a dedicated team that combines old-fashioned craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver work that dentists and patients trust.
