Our Services

Precision Dental Solutions

From emergency repairs to digital scanning, we provide a comprehensive range of services for both patients and dental professionals.

Cosmetic Dentures

Beautiful, natural-looking cosmetic dentures tailored to each patient. Includes copy dentures, single tooth replacements, and full sets crafted with over 35 years of expertise.

Crown & Bridge Work

Precision crown and bridge solutions for dental professionals. Using the latest digital scanning technology introduced in 2024, we deliver accurate results with a fast turnaround.

Emergency Repairs

Simple repairs completed while you wait — within one hour. We offer a 24-hour emergency telephone service and a 3-month guarantee on all repairs for total peace of mind.

Flexible Dentures (Valplast)

Lightweight, comfortable Valplast flexible dentures that blend naturally with your gums. A metal-free alternative that offers superior comfort and aesthetics.

Cobalt Chrome Dentures

Strong, durable cobalt chrome frameworks providing excellent retention and comfort. Precision-engineered for dental professionals with daily collection and delivery service.

Digital Scanning

New in 2024 — cutting-edge digital scanning technology for dental practices. Faster, more accurate impressions for better-fitting prosthetics and orthodontic solutions.

About Us

A Family Legacy in Dental Craftsmanship

Founded in 1989 by Chris Humphreys, Manchester Dental Lab has grown from a small family workshop into one of Manchester's most trusted dental laboratories. With over 30 years of hands-on experience as a Dental Technician and qualified Clinical Dental Technician since 2014, Chris brings unmatched expertise to every case.

The tradition continues with Ryan Humphreys, who qualified as a CDT in 2025, carrying the family's commitment to precision and patient care into the next generation.
