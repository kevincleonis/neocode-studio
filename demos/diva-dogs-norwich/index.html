About Us

More Than a Grooming Salon

At Diva Dogs Norwich, we believe every dog deserves to feel like a star. We're not just groomers, we're pet lovers who treat your furry family member with the same care and attention you would at home.

Based in Shipfield, Norwich, our calm and welcoming salon is designed to make even the most nervous pups feel at ease. We use premium, dog-safe products and take the time to get things right.

Our Services

Pamper Packages for Every Pup

From a quick tidy-up to the full diva experience, we've got your dog covered. Every service includes loads of love and treats.

🛀

Full Groom

The complete pamper: bath, blow-dry, brush out, haircut, ear clean, nail trim and a spritz of cologne. Your pup will leave looking and smelling incredible.

🚿

Bath & Brush

A thorough wash with premium shampoo, professional blow-dry, full brush out and nail clip. Perfect for dogs who don't need a cut but need freshening up.

🐶

Puppy Introduction

A gentle first experience for puppies to get comfortable with grooming. We go at their pace with lots of praise and treats. Building trust from day one.

✂️

Nail Clipping

Quick, stress-free nail trimming to keep your dog's paws healthy and comfortable. We handle even the most reluctant paws with patience and care.

💫

De-shedding Treatment

Specialist treatment for heavy shedders. We use professional de-shedding tools and techniques to remove loose undercoat and keep your home fur-free.

🌟

Diva Spa Package

The ultimate luxury experience. Full groom plus deep conditioning treatment, blueberry facial, paw balm and a bandana. Because your dog IS the main character.
