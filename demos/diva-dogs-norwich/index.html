Our Services

Everything Your Pup Needs to Shine

From full grooms to quick nail trims, we've got your dog covered. Every service includes lots of cuddles (that's non-negotiable).

✄

Full Groom

The complete diva package. Bath, blow-dry, full clip or hand-strip, ears cleaned, nails trimmed, and a spritz of cologne. They'll strut out feeling brand new.

🐶

Puppy Intro

A gentle first experience designed to build your puppy's confidence. We'll take it slow with a mini bath, light trim, and plenty of positive vibes. Perfect for pups under 16 weeks.

🚿

Bath & Brush

Not quite ready for a full groom? No worries. A deep shampoo, condition, blow-dry and thorough brush-out to keep your dog's coat healthy and fresh between grooms.

💅

Nail Trim

Quick, stress-free nail trims to keep those paws in top shape. We're gentle and patient, even with the wiggly ones. Walk-ins welcome when we've got availability.

🧺

De-Matting

Got a tangled situation? We'll carefully work through mats and knots with minimal stress. We always prioritise your dog's comfort and will chat through the best approach first.

🦷

Teeth Cleaning

Fresh breath and healthy gums! Our non-invasive teeth cleaning helps reduce plaque and tartar build-up. It's a game-changer for dogs who aren't fans of the toothbrush at home.
