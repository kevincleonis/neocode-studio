Our Services

Grooming That Dogs Love

Every dog gets one-to-one attention from start to finish. Relaxing music, gentle handling, and plenty of cuddles included as standard.

Full Groom

A complete pamper session including bath, blow-dry, full clip, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and styling. Your dog will leave looking and feeling their absolute best.

Puppy's First Groom

A gentle, stress-free introduction to grooming for puppies. We take extra time and care to ensure your pup has a positive experience that sets them up for a lifetime of easy grooming.

Clipping & Styling

Professional clipping for all breeds. Whether it's a breed-standard cut or a practical trim, we tailor every clip to suit your dog's coat type, lifestyle, and your preferences.

Bath & Blow-Dry

A refreshing bath with premium shampoos suited to your dog's coat, followed by a thorough blow-dry. Perfect between full grooms to keep your dog clean and fresh.

Nail Trimming

Safe and comfortable nail trimming to keep your dog's paws healthy. Overgrown nails can cause pain and mobility issues — regular trims keep your dog happy on their feet.

Spa & Pamper

The ultimate treat for your furry friend. Our spa experience includes a luxury bath, conditioning treatment, massage, and finishing touches. Because every dog deserves to feel special.

About Us

Where Every Dog Gets the VIP Treatment

At Paws & Claws Dog Grooming, we believe every dog deserves to be treated with the same love and care you'd give them at home — if not more. That's why we never use cages. Your dog receives one-to-one attention from the moment they arrive until the moment they leave.

We play soothing music to keep dogs relaxed, and our fully qualified groomers treat every dog as if they were our own. Based at 2 Holtdale Croft in Leeds, we're proud to be a trusted name for dog owners across the city.

  • No cages — dogs are never left alone
  • One-to-one attention at all times
  • Calming music for a stress-free experience
  • Fully qualified and fully insured
  • Every dog treated as our own
