About Us
We Genuinely Love What We Do

At Paws & Claws Dog Grooming, every day is a good day because we get to spend it with dogs. Based at 2 Holtdale Croft in Leeds LS16, we've built our reputation on trust, gentle care, and outstanding grooming results.

Our fully qualified groomers believe grooming should never be stressful for your dog. That's why we created a cage-free environment with soothing music, one-to-one attention, and more cuddles than you can count. We use high-end shampoos and conditioners suited to each coat type.

  • No cages - your dog is never left alone
  • One-to-one attention at all times
  • Calming music throughout every groom
  • Fully qualified and fully insured
  • Premium specialist shampoos for every coat
  • Every dog treated as if they were our own
