Our Promise to You
What Makes Us Different
We don't just groom dogs — we pamper them. Every detail of our service is designed to keep your dog calm, comfortable, and happy.

No Cages, Ever

Your dog is never placed in a cage or kennel. We believe cages create stress and anxiety. Dogs roam freely in our safe, supervised space.

One-to-One Attention

Every dog gets dedicated one-to-one care from arrival to departure. Your dog is never left unattended — not even for a moment.

Calming Music

We play soothing music throughout every grooming session. It keeps dogs relaxed and helps create a spa-like atmosphere they enjoy.

Treated As Our Own

We look after and pamper your baby as if they were our own. Lots of cuddles, gentle handling, and genuine love for every dog.

Fully Qualified & Insured

Professional, trained groomers with full insurance coverage. You can trust us with your most precious family member.

All Breeds Welcome

From tiny Pomeranians to large Golden Retrievers, we welcome every breed with the same level of care and expertise.

About Us

We Genuinely Love What We Do

At Paws & Claws Dog Grooming, every day is a good day because we get to spend it with dogs. Based at 2 Holtdale Croft in Leeds, we've built our reputation on trust, gentle care, and outstanding grooming results.

Our fully qualified groomers believe that grooming should never be stressful for your dog. That's why we created a cage-free environment with soothing music, one-to-one attention, and more cuddles than you can count.

  • No cages — dogs are never left alone
  • One-to-one attention at all times
  • Calming music for a stress-free experience
  • Fully qualified and fully insured
