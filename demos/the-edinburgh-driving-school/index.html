● About Us

Your Instructor, Your Pace

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all lessons. Every learner's different, and your instruction should be too.

Patient, Professional, and Fully Qualified

At The Edinburgh Driving School, we're passionate about helping learners feel comfortable behind the wheel. Our DVSA-approved instructor brings years of experience and a calm, encouraging teaching style that builds real confidence.

We don't rush you. Whether it's your first time turning the ignition or you're prepping for test day, you'll always feel supported and in control.

  • ✓ DVSA-approved and fully qualified instructor
  • ✓ Dual-control, well-maintained modern car
  • ✓ Flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends
  • ✓ Lessons tailored to your learning speed
  • ✓ Pick-up and drop-off from your door
● Lesson Types

Find the Right Lesson for You

From total beginners to experienced drivers looking to brush up, we've got a lesson type that'll suit you perfectly.

🚗

Beginner Lessons

Starting from scratch? No worries. We'll take you from zero experience to test-ready with a structured, step-by-step approach that covers everything from clutch control to roundabouts.

⚡

Intensive Courses

Need to pass quickly? Our intensive courses pack everything into a condensed time frame. Perfect if you've got a deadline or just want to get on the road fast.

🔄

Refresher Lessons

Already passed but haven't driven in a while? We'll help you shake off the rust and rebuild your confidence behind the wheel. No judgement, just support.

🛣

Motorway Lessons

Motorways can feel intimidating. We'll teach you how to merge, lane change, and handle high-speed driving safely. You'll wonder why you were ever nervous.

🏆

Pass Plus

Just passed your test? Pass Plus gives you extra skills and experience across different road types and conditions. Plus, it can lower your insurance premiums.

📋

Test Preparation

Test day approaching? We'll run through mock tests, cover common routes around Edinburgh test centres, and make sure you're feeling calm and ready.
