Our Story

Where Vintage Charm Meets Modern Beauty

Tucked away on Front Street in Acomb, Shabby To Chic isn't just a salon. It's an experience. We've built a space where you can sit back, relax, and let our talented team bring out the very best version of you.

From bespoke hair styling to indulgent beauty treatments, every visit is tailored to what you actually want. No cookie-cutter approaches here. Just honest advice, skilled hands, and a warm cup of tea waiting for you.
