What We Do

Electrical Solutions for Every Sector

Whether you need a full rewire, CCTV installation, or industrial power management, our team of 24 certified engineers has you covered.

Wiring & Installations

Complete electrical wiring for new builds, extensions, and renovations. BS7671 compliant installations for commercial, industrial, and residential properties across Greater Manchester.

CCTV & Security Systems

Professional CCTV setup, door entry systems, and intercom installations. Protect your premises with high-definition security solutions designed and installed by our qualified team.

Testing & Inspection

NICEIC periodic inspection reports, electrical condition reports (EICR), and portable appliance testing (PAT). Keep your property safe and legally compliant.

Lighting Design & LED

Energy-efficient lighting design for offices, warehouses, retail spaces, and homes. Reduce your energy bills with modern LED solutions and smart lighting controls.

Data & IT Infrastructure

Structured cabling, data communications, server room power, and network infrastructure. From Cat6 installations to full data centre fit-outs for modern business needs.

Emergency Callouts

24/7 emergency electrical services across Manchester. Fault finding, power outage response, and urgent repairs. Our rapid response team is always on standby when you need us most.

Fire Alarm Systems

Design, installation, and maintenance of fire alarm systems and emergency lighting. Fully compliant with BS 5839 standards to keep your building and occupants safe.

Industrial Power Systems

Power factor correction, 240/400V power supplies, control panels, automated systems, and machinery relocation. Specialists in high-security environments including airports and railway stations.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Outdated fuse board? We upgrade consumer units to the latest 18th Edition standards with RCD protection. Essential for safety in older Manchester properties.

About DMS Electrical

Built on Expertise. Driven by Standards.

Established in 2019 by David Smith, DMS Electrical Engineering Limited has rapidly become one of Manchester's most trusted electrical contractors.

Operating from our base at Cariocca Business Park in Ardwick, our team of 24 skilled and certified electricians serves clients across the North West. From high-security installations at major UK airports and railway stations to domestic rewires in Didsbury, we bring the same level of professionalism to every job. Client expectations have been consistently exceeded as we've built a solid reputation for delivering results within budget and on deadline.
