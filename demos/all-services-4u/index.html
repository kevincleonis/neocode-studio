What We Do

Every Trade, One Team

Whether it's an emergency lockout at midnight or a full bathroom refit, our multi-skilled team handles it all so you don't have to.

Locksmith

Locked out? We'll get you back in fast. Lock changes, emergency access, UPVC mechanisms, and security upgrades for homes and businesses.

Electrician

From faulty sockets to full rewires, our qualified electricians handle it all. Lighting, fuse boards, fault finding, and safety inspections.

Plumber

Leaking taps, burst pipes, boiler issues, bathroom installations. We'll sort your plumbing problems quickly and cleanly.

Handyman

Odd jobs piling up? Shelving, painting, tiling, flat-pack assembly, TV mounting. No job's too small for our handyman team.

Carpentry

Custom shelving, door hanging, skirting boards, decking, and bespoke woodwork. Quality craftsmanship that lasts.

General Repairs

Gutters, fencing, roofing patches, window repairs, damp fixes. We handle the things that other tradespeople won't bother with.
