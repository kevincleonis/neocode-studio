Our Services

Everything Your Car Needs,
Right Where You Are

From full diagnostics to brake replacements, Ricky handles it all without you having to leave your driveway.

Diagnostics

Professional OBD diagnostics to find out exactly what's wrong. We'll read fault codes, run live data checks, and give you a clear explanation of every issue.

Full Servicing

Interim and full services carried out on your driveway. Oil change, filter replacements, fluid top-ups, and a thorough multi-point inspection included.

Brakes

Pads, discs, callipers, and brake fluid changes. Don't compromise on safety. We'll get your stopping power back to where it should be.

Clutch Repairs

Clutch feeling heavy or slipping? We'll diagnose the problem and carry out full clutch replacements, including the flywheel if needed.

Electrical

Battery testing, alternator checks, starter motor issues, and wiring faults. If it's an electrical gremlin, we'll track it down.

Pre-MOT Check

Don't waste money on a failed MOT. We'll run through everything the tester looks at and fix any issues before you book in, so you pass first time.
