Look After Your Ink

AFTERCARE GUIDE

Getting tattooed is only half the journey. Proper aftercare makes the difference between good ink and great ink.

01

KEEP IT COVERED

Leave the wrap on for 2-4 hours after your session. This protects the fresh tattoo from bacteria while the initial healing begins.

02

CLEAN GENTLY

Wash with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap. Pat dry with a clean paper towel. No rubbing, no scrubbing, no soaking.

03

MOISTURISE DAILY

Apply a thin layer of tattoo-specific balm or fragrance-free moisturiser 2-3 times daily. Keep the skin hydrated but don't drown it.

04

PROTECT & HEAL

Avoid direct sunlight, swimming, and tight clothing for at least 2 weeks. Don't pick or scratch. Let your body do the work.
