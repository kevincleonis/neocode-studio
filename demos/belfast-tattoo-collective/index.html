Our Story

MORE THAN INK. IT'S A CRAFT.

Belfast Tattoo Collective isn't your average tattoo shop. We're a tight-knit group of artists who came together with one shared belief: that every tattoo deserves the same care and precision as a piece of fine art.

Located right in the heart of Belfast, we've been turning ideas into permanent artwork for over a decade. Whether it's your first piece or you're building a full sleeve, you'll feel at home here. We take the time to understand your vision before the needle ever touches skin.

Walk-ins are welcome, but we always recommend booking a consultation so we can give your piece the attention it deserves.

Post-Session

AFTERCARE TIPS

Getting the tattoo is half the journey. Proper aftercare is essential for clean healing and vibrant colours that last.

01
KEEP IT WRAPPED

Leave the cling film or second-skin bandage on for the time your artist recommends, usually 2 to 4 hours. This protects the fresh wound from bacteria and friction.

02
WASH GENTLY

Use lukewarm water and a fragrance-free antibacterial soap. Gently clean the area with your fingertips, never a cloth or sponge. Pat dry with a clean paper towel.

03
MOISTURISE REGULARLY

Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser or a dedicated tattoo balm 2 to 3 times daily. Don't overdo it though, your skin still needs to breathe.

04
AVOID SUN & WATER

No swimming, saunas, or prolonged sun exposure for at least 2 to 3 weeks. UV rays fade fresh ink quickly. Once healed, always use SPF 50 on your tattoo outdoors.

05
DON'T SCRATCH OR PICK

Your tattoo will peel and itch as it heals. That's completely normal. Resist the urge to scratch or pick at it. Let the skin shed naturally for the best result.

06
WEAR LOOSE CLOTHING

Tight clothes can rub against fresh ink and cause irritation. Opt for loose, breathable fabrics over the tattooed area while it heals, especially during the first week.
