Your Local Garage, Done Right

Mototech Garage is a family-run repair centre based in the heart of Portsmouth. We've built our reputation on straight talk, quality workmanship, and prices that won't make your eyes water.

Whether it's your annual MOT, a full service, or something that doesn't sound quite right under the bonnet, we've got you covered.

  • DVSA approved MOT testing station
  • Experienced, qualified technicians
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • All makes and models welcome

Our Services

From routine MOTs to complex diagnostics, we handle it all under one roof.

📋

MOT Testing

Quick, thorough MOT testing by certified testers. We'll walk you through any advisories and never push unnecessary work.

🔧

Full Service

Comprehensive interim and full services to keep your car running smooth. Oil, filters, fluids, and a full health check.

⚫

Brakes

Pads, discs, calipers, and fluid. We'll sort your brakes quickly so you can stop safely, every time.

💻

Diagnostics

Engine warning light on? Our diagnostic equipment reads fault codes across all makes so we find the real issue fast.

💨

Exhaust

From catalytic converters to back boxes, we supply and fit exhausts for all vehicles. Louder than usual? Bring it in.

⬤

Tyres

New tyres supplied and fitted at competitive prices. We also do puncture repairs, balancing, and alignment checks.

❄

Air Conditioning

Full A/C regas and leak testing. Don't sweat through another summer with a dodgy air con system.
