What We Do

Expert Services for Every Vehicle

From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we've got the tools and the know-how to get you back on the road fast.

MOT Testing

DVSA-approved MOT testing station. Class 4 and 7 tests with free retests within 10 working days. We'll walk you through any advisories.

Full Servicing

Interim and full service options following manufacturer guidelines. Oil, filters, fluids, brakes, and a comprehensive multi-point inspection.

Diagnostics

State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to identify faults quickly and accurately. Engine management, ABS, airbag, and emissions diagnostics.

Engine Repairs

From timing belts to head gaskets, we handle all engine work. Petrol and diesel specialists with decades of hands-on experience.

Brakes & Clutch

Brake pad and disc replacement, clutch repairs and full hydraulic system work. We don't cut corners when it comes to your safety.

Air Con & Electrical

Air conditioning regas and repair, plus full electrical diagnostics. Alternators, starters, wiring faults, and battery testing.

MOT Testing Centre

DVSA-Approved MOT Station

We're a fully approved MOT testing facility for Class 4 and Class 7 vehicles. Our testers are trained, certified, and up to date with the latest DVSA standards.

Class 4 MOT (cars and light vans up to 3,000kg)
Class 7 MOT (vans and goods vehicles 3,000-3,500kg)
Free retest within 10 working days if booked with us
While-you-wait appointments available
Pre-MOT checks to avoid nasty surprises

MOT Reminder Service
