Our Story

A Family of Jewellers in the Heart of Ledbury

Past and Present is the work of husband-and-wife team Annette and Steve Crowe. Annette brings over 30 years of passion and expertise in antique jewellery, advising customers on everything from Victorian brooches to family heirlooms. Steve is the craftsman, running the on-site workshop where he creates bespoke pieces and handles all repairs by hand.

Customers describe visiting as "like calling on friends." That's exactly how Annette and Steve want it: no pressure, just genuine knowledge, honest advice, and beautiful jewellery.

Services

More Than Just a Shop

With an on-site workshop and decades of expertise, we offer a complete jewellery service under one roof.

Jewellery Repairs

Ring resizing, chain repairs, stone setting, re-polishing, and restoration. All done in-house by Steve, never sent away. Your pieces stay safe with us.

Bespoke Commissions

Have something unique made just for you. Steve designs and crafts bespoke gold and silver pieces in his workshop. Engagement rings, anniversary gifts, memorial jewellery.

Valuations

Professional valuations for insurance, probate, and personal purposes. Annette's 30+ years of antique expertise ensures accurate, trusted assessments.

We Buy Gold & Silver

Sell your unused, broken, or unwanted gold and silver. Fair prices, honest assessment, immediate payment. No obligation.
