Our Services

Complete Electrical Solutions for Every Need

From quick repairs to full rewires, we deliver safe, certified work for homes, businesses and industrial properties across Teesside.

Fault Finding & Repairs

Quick diagnosis and reliable fixes for any electrical issue. Tripping circuits, flickering lights, power outages - we'll sort it fast, usually in a single visit.

Lighting Installation

Interior and exterior lighting design and installation. From LED downlights and feature lighting to garden and security lights that transform your space.

Consumer Units & Fuse Boards

Outdated fuse board? We'll upgrade you to a modern, BS 7671 compliant consumer unit with RCD protection - keeping your family and property safe.

Full & Partial Rewires

Safe, tidy rewiring for older properties that need updating. We minimise disruption and make sure everything meets current regulations before signing off.

EICR & Electrical Testing

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for landlords, homeowners and businesses. Full inspection, testing and certification to meet your legal obligations.

Smoke & Fire Alarms

Hardwired smoke detector and fire alarm systems for homes and commercial properties. Fully compliant installations that could save lives and meet insurance requirements.

About Ian
