Our Story

Three Decades of Making Cheltenham Look Good

Hair Connexion isn't just a salon. It's a Cheltenham institution. For over 30 years, Alison Davis and her team have been welcoming locals through the doors at 13 Bennington Street, delivering cuts and colour that keep people coming back.

Whether you're after a fresh restyle, a classic shampoo and set, or a full colour transformation, you'll find a warm, relaxed space where the stylists actually listen to what you want.
