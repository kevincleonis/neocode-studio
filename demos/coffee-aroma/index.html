Our Story

Crafted With Passion, Served With Love

Since 2005, Coffee Aroma has been Lincoln's home for exceptional coffee. We offer the widest selection of single-origin coffees in the region, with our espresso blend changing seasonally as different producing regions come into harvest.

Our baristas are trained to championship standards, ensuring every cup is crafted with precision and care. Everything is baked fresh daily - first batch at 8am, second around 10:30am.
