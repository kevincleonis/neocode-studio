Services
Complete Piano Care

From a routine tune-up to full restoration, your piano is in expert hands.

🎵

Piano Tuning

Precision tuning to concert pitch (A440) for uprights and grands. Regular tuning keeps your piano sounding beautiful and extends its life.

⚙️

Regulation

Action regulation to restore touch and responsiveness. Adjusting hammers, dampers and keys for an even, satisfying playing experience.

🔧

Repairs

Broken strings, sticking keys, pedal problems and worn hammers. We diagnose and fix mechanical issues to keep your piano playing smoothly.

🎹

Voicing

Hammer voicing to shape your piano's tone — brighter, warmer, more even. Subtle adjustments that transform your instrument's character.

🏫

Schools & Venues

Contract tuning for schools, churches, concert halls and recording studios. Flexible scheduling, competitive rates for regular maintenance.

📋

Pre-Purchase Advice

Thinking of buying a piano? We inspect second-hand instruments and advise on condition, value and any work needed before you commit.

About Jim

A Lifetime Dedicated to the Piano

With over 25 years of experience, Jim Denton is one of the most trusted piano tuners in the region. Trained in the traditional art of aural tuning, Jim combines deep technical knowledge with a genuine passion for music.

From family uprights to Steinway grands, every piano receives the same meticulous attention. Jim works with private clients, schools, churches and professional musicians to keep their instruments in peak condition.
