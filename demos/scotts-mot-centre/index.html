What We Do

Everything Your Car Needs

From your annual MOT to major repairs, we've got the skills, the tools and the experience to get it sorted.

MOT Testing

Full DVSA-approved MOT testing for Class 4 vehicles. We'll let you know exactly what's needed to get your car through, with no pressure to have the work done here.

Servicing

Interim and full services to keep your car running smoothly. We use quality parts and follow manufacturer guidelines so your warranty stays intact.

Repairs

General mechanical repairs for all makes and models. Whether it's a worn clutch, a noisy exhaust or something more complex, we'll get it fixed properly.

Diagnostics

Modern diagnostic equipment to pinpoint engine faults, warning lights and electrical issues. We'll find the problem fast and explain everything in plain English.

Tyres

New tyres supplied and fitted at competitive prices. We'll check your tread depth, advise on the best options for your budget and get them on the same day.

Brakes

Brake pads, discs, callipers and fluid changes. Your safety's our priority, so we won't cut corners. We'll always show you what needs replacing and why.
