Our Story

Family Run. Locally Sourced. Always Fresh.

Westdene Butchers has been at the heart of the community for years, providing quality meat and personal service that supermarkets simply can't match.

We work directly with local farms to ensure every cut meets our exacting standards. Our butchers are trained in traditional methods, hand-cutting every order with precision and care.

Whether you're after a quick midweek dinner or planning a special Sunday roast, we're here to help you choose the perfect cut and give you cooking tips to get the best from it.
