Our Specialities

Dining Experiences & Signature Dishes

From sizzling tandoori platters to aromatic biryanis, discover the rich flavours that have made us Alsager's most loved restaurant.

Tandoori Specials

Marinated overnight in yoghurt and aromatic spices, our tandoori dishes are chargrilled to smoky perfection in our traditional clay oven.

Biryani Collection

Fragrant basmati rice layered with tender meats and infused with saffron, cardamom, and rose water. A feast for all the senses.

Chef's Specials

Exclusive creations by our head chef featuring unique flavour combinations and premium ingredients not found on the regular menu.

Banquet Menu

Perfect for celebrations and group dining. Multi-course set menus featuring the best of our kitchen at exceptional value.

Buffet Nights

Unlimited helpings of our finest dishes at special evening buffets. A changing selection ensuring there is always something new to try.

Delivery & Takeaway
