What We Do

Full Workshop Service,
Right at Your Door

From routine servicing to complex diagnostics, we carry the tools and parts in our fully-stocked van. All makes and models covered.

Full Servicing

Interim and full service packages for all makes and models. Oil, filters, fluids and multi-point inspection included.

Engine Diagnostics

Latest diagnostic equipment to pinpoint faults quickly. Dashboard warning lights, error codes and performance issues sorted on the spot.

Timing Belts

Cambelt and timing chain replacement using OE-spec parts. Don't risk engine damage from a snapped belt. We fit them at your home.

Brakes & Discs

Brake pads, discs and caliper servicing. We check your entire braking system and replace what's needed, right on your drive.

Clutch Repairs

Clutch replacement, slave cylinders and flywheel inspection. Save hundreds compared to main dealer prices.

Breakdowns

Stranded at home or at work? We'll come to you, diagnose the problem and get you moving again. Fast response across South Manchester.

About CPM

Family-Run, Locally Trusted, Properly Qualified

CPM Mobile Vehicle Repairs is a family-run business based in Sale, Cheshire. We've been working in the motor trade for over 18 years, building a reputation on honest work and fair pricing.

We're not a big chain and we don't upsell you parts you don't need. When we find a problem, we explain it in plain English, give you a clear price, and only go ahead with your say-so. That's how we've kept customers coming back for nearly two decades.
