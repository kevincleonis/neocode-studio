About Us

We Don't Just Fix Cars. We Build Trust.

At Newnham Street Garage, we believe you shouldn't have to worry about being overcharged or getting work done that isn't needed. We'll always give you an honest assessment, explain what's going on, and let you decide.

Whether it's your annual MOT, a routine service, or something that's been making a funny noise for weeks, we've got the skills and the tools to sort it out. We work on all makes and models, and we won't rest until the job's done right.

What We Do

Services You Can Count On

From quick MOTs to full engine repairs, we've got every angle covered. Here's what we can do for you.

MOT Testing

We're an authorised MOT testing centre. We'll check everything thoroughly and give you a clear report. If something needs sorting, we'll talk you through your options first.

Servicing

Regular servicing keeps your car running smoothly and holds its value. We offer interim and full services tailored to your vehicle's needs and mileage.

Repairs

Brakes, clutches, exhausts, suspension... you name it, we can fix it. We use quality parts and we'll always give you a quote before we start any work.

Diagnostics

Got a warning light on your dashboard? Our diagnostic equipment can read fault codes and pinpoint the problem, so we can get it fixed quickly and accurately.
