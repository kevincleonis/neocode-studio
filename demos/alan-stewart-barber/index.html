About Alan

Trained By The Best.
Qualified At The Top.

Alan was originally taught by a master barber with over 40 years of experience in the craft. He went on to earn his NVQ Level 3 Diploma in Barbering with a Distinction — the highest mark possible.

As Telford's only dedicated mobile barber, Alan combines the right skills with a friendly, courteous approach. All you need to provide is a well-lit area and a power outlet — he handles everything else, including the clean-up.
