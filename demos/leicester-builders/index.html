What We Do

Comprehensive Building Services

From the foundations to the finishing touches, Leicester Builders delivers expert craftsmanship across every aspect of construction.

Extensions

Single and double-storey extensions designed to maximise your living space. From kitchen-diners to annexes, we handle planning through to completion.

Loft Conversions

Transform wasted attic space into stunning bedrooms, offices or playrooms. Dormer, hip-to-gable and Velux conversions available.

Renovations

Complete home renovations that breathe new life into tired properties. Structural alterations, modernisations and full interior refits.

New Builds

Ground-up construction of residential and commercial properties. Managed from architectural plans to final handover with meticulous attention to detail.

Plastering & Brickwork

Expert internal plastering and external brickwork including pointing, rendering and decorative masonry. Clean, professional finishes every time.

Roofing & Groundwork

Complete roofing solutions from new installations to repairs, plus foundations, drainage and all groundwork to get your project started right.
