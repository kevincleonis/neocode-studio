Our Services

Grooming Tailored to Every Breed

Professional grooming services designed to keep your dog healthy, comfortable and looking fantastic.

Full Groom

Complete grooming package including bath, blow-dry, full clip, nail trim, ear clean and finishing spritz. Your dog leaves looking show-ready.

Bath & Brush

Thorough wash with premium shampoo, conditioner, professional blow-dry and a full brush-out. Perfect for short-haired or low-maintenance breeds.

Puppy First Groom

Gentle, stress-free introduction for puppies. We build confidence with calm handling, a mini bath, light trim and lots of positive reinforcement.

Hand Stripping

Specialist technique for wire-coated breeds. Maintains natural coat texture, colour and weather-resistant properties. Essential for terriers and spaniels.

Nail Clipping

Quick, safe nail trimming to keep your dog comfortable and protect your floors. Can be booked as a standalone service or add-on to any groom.

De-matting & Breed Cuts

Careful de-matting without causing discomfort, plus breed-specific styling that respects your dog's coat type and conformation standards.
