Our Services
Meticulous Care for Every Timepiece

Whether your clock needs a simple service or a full restoration, we treat every piece with the respect it deserves.

🔧

Full Restoration

Complete mechanical restoration including movement overhaul, dial refinishing, case repair and period-correct replacement parts.

⚙️

Movement Service

Thorough cleaning, oiling and regulation of clock movements. Worn pivots repaired, mainsprings replaced, escapements adjusted.

🪵

Case Restoration

Sympathetic repair and refinishing of wooden cases, brass work, glass replacement and French polishing to preserve original character.

🔔

Chime & Strike

Repair and synchronisation of striking trains, Westminster chimes, cuckoo mechanisms and musical movements.

🏠

House Calls

For grandfather clocks and large wall clocks, we come to you. Collection and delivery service available across Cornwall.

📋

Valuations

Professional clock valuations for insurance, probate and sale. Expert assessment of age, origin, condition and market value.
