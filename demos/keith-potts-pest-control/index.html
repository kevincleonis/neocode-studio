About Us

Four Generations of Expertise

A Family Legacy in Pest Control

Keith Potts is the fourth generation in his family to work in pest control and game keeping, with knowledge passed down from his grandfather and uncle across decades.

Originally from Northumberland, Keith has been based in Inverness for over 30 years, building an unmatched understanding of Highland pest challenges — from rural estates to city-centre businesses.

As an NPTA member and CRRU/Think Wildlife associate, every treatment follows the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.
