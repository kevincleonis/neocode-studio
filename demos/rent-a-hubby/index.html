Meet Brian

Your Local, Friendly Handyman

Brian Lovegrove is the man behind Rent A Hubby — Brighton & Hove's most trusted handyman. With his distinctive branded van and professional uniform, you'll know help has arrived. No job is too small.

  • Fully insured with £1 million public liability cover
  • Charges rounded to the nearest quarter-hour — you only pay for what you use
