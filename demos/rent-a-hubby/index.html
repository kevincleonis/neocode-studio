Meet Brian

The Friendly Face Behind Your Repairs

Brian Lovegrove started Rent A Hubby with a simple idea: give people a handyman they can actually rely on. No call-out charges, no disappearing halfway through a job, no dodgy quotes. Just honest, friendly, professional work at a fair price.

Based in Newhaven and covering all of East and West Sussex, Brian and the team have built a reputation for turning up when they say they will, doing the job properly, and leaving your home tidy. It's not rocket science, it's just how things should be done.
