Meet Your Instructor

Hi, I'm Hayley

I've spent over a decade helping people transform their relationship with movement. My journey started with a simple belief: everyone deserves to feel strong, calm, and at home in their body.

I'm a fully qualified Pilates and yoga instructor with certifications in mat pilates, reformer pilates, vinyasa flow, yin yoga, and prenatal movement. My classes aren't about being perfect. They're about showing up, breathing deep, and leaving feeling better than when you walked in.

Classes for Every Body
