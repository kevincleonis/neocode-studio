About Us

Your Local Garage in Ketley, Telford

Autocare Telford has been looking after vehicles across Telford and Shropshire for years. Based at Unit 10 on Ketley Business Park, our team brings over 30 years of combined experience to every job that comes through the doors.

We're not just about oil changes and brake pads. We specialise in sports suspension work and performance exhaust upgrades, so whether you need a routine service or want to give your car a serious boost, you're in the right place.

We pride ourselves on honest advice and transparent pricing. We'll always keep you updated while your car's with us, and we'll never do extra work without your approval. Need a quote? We'll match or beat any competitor.
