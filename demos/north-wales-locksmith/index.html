What We Do

Our Locksmith Services

From emergency lockouts to full security upgrades, we've got the skills and the gear to handle any job across North Wales.

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your home, car or business? We'll get you back in quickly and safely, usually without any damage to your door or lock. Available 24/7, every single day.

Lock Replacement

Whether your lock's worn out, been forced or you just want a fresh start after moving house, we'll fit high-quality replacement locks from trusted brands like Yale and ERA.

UPVC Door & Window Locks

UPVC mechanisms can be tricky, but we deal with them every day. We'll repair or replace multipoint locking systems, handles, euro cylinders and more.

Commercial Locksmith

We work with shops, offices, pubs and warehouses across North Wales. Master key systems, access control, panic bars and high-security locks all fitted to your spec.

Security Upgrades

Want better peace of mind? We'll assess your property and recommend upgrades like anti-snap cylinders, British Standard locks and security chains that actually work.

Key Cutting

Need spare keys? We cut keys on-site for most door locks, padlocks and window locks. We can also supply restricted key systems if you want extra control over who has copies.
