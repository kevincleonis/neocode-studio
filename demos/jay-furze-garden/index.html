Expert Advice

Seasonal Garden Tips

Keep your garden looking great all year with these quick tips from Jay.

🌸

Spring

It's time to get out there. Start by clearing winter debris, feed your lawn, and get your borders prepped for planting. Don't forget to prune your roses before new growth kicks in.

☀️

Summer

Water early morning or late evening to avoid evaporation. Mow regularly but don't cut too short. Deadhead flowers to encourage more blooms and keep on top of weeds.

🍂

Autumn

Rake up fallen leaves before they smother your lawn. Plant spring bulbs now for a colourful display later. It's also the perfect time to aerate and overseed patchy areas.

❄️

Winter

Protect tender plants with fleece or mulch. Clean and sharpen your tools so they're ready for spring. Plan ahead and think about any changes you'd like to make next year.
