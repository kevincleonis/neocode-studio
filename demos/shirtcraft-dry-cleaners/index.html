About Rob

Garment care that's personal, not corporate.

Rob started Shirtcraft because he believed something simple: your clothes should come back looking better than when you dropped them off. Not just clean. Better.

That's still the standard today. Every garment gets individual attention, proper care for its fabric type, and the kind of finishing you won't get from the big chains. Whether it's a business shirt you wear every Monday or a wedding dress you'll keep for a lifetime, we treat it like it matters. Because it does.

What We Do

Services built around your wardrobe.

We don't cut corners. Every item is inspected, treated for stains, cleaned with the right method, and finished by hand.

👕

Dry Cleaning

Professional solvent-based cleaning for suits, dresses, coats, and delicate fabrics. We'll get out stains you didn't think were possible.

👔

Shirt Service

Our signature service. Laundered, pressed, and finished on hangers or folded. Crisp collars and perfect creases, every single time.

💍

Wedding Dresses

Specialist bridal gown cleaning and preservation. We'll handle your dress with the care and attention it deserves, pre or post-wedding.

🏠

Curtains & Drapes

Take-down, clean, re-hang. We handle curtains, drapes, and soft furnishings without the hassle. They'll look brand new again.

✂

Alterations

Hems, waist adjustments, zip replacements, and tailoring tweaks. If it doesn't fit right, we'll sort it out for you.

🏢

Corporate Accounts

Regular collection and delivery for businesses. Staff uniforms, hotel linen, restaurant table wear. Volume pricing and reliable scheduling.
