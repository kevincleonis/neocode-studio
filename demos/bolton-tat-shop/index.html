What We Do

Every Style. Every Vision.

From fine line script to full colour sleeves, our artists cover every tattoo style imaginable. Walk in with an idea, walk out with art you'll wear forever.

✎

Custom Design

Bring your concept, reference, or even just a rough idea. We'll sketch it, refine it, and make sure it's perfect before any ink touches skin. Consultations are always free.

✻

Realism & Portraits

Photo-realistic tattoos that capture every detail. Whether it's a loved one, a pet, or a tribute piece, our artists bring images to life with incredible depth and precision.

☯

Traditional & Neo-Trad

Bold lines, vibrant colours, and classic designs that stand the test of time. From old school anchors to modern neo-traditional pieces with rich colour palettes.

❖

Black & Grey

Smooth shading, dramatic contrast, and timeless elegance. Our black and grey work ranges from delicate fine line to bold, statement-making pieces.

✿

Cover-Ups & Reworks

Got an old tattoo you've outgrown? We specialise in creative cover-ups and reworks that transform existing ink into something you'll actually love showing off.

♦

Professional Piercing

Male and female piercers on hand for everything from lobes to more advanced placements. Sterile environment, premium jewellery, and expert aftercare advice.

Meet The Team

Award-Winning Artists

Our crew brings decades of combined experience and a genuine passion for the craft. Each artist has their own speciality, but they're all comfortable working across any style you throw at them.

Gary Parkinson

Owner / Lead Artist

Award-winning tattooist and the driving force behind The Bolton Tat Shop. Over a decade of experience in custom work across every style, with a particular eye for detail in realism and portraits.

Matt McHugh

Artist

Versatile artist with a strong background in traditional and neo-traditional styles. Matt's bold line work and colour theory bring every piece to life with striking impact.

Alex Kelly

Artist

Specialising in black and grey work, Alex has a talent for creating atmospheric, moody pieces with incredibly smooth shading and sharp detail work.
