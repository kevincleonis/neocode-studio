Who We Are
More Than Just
A Tattoo Studio

A Comfortable & Friendly Atmosphere

For over 11 years, The Bolton Tat Shop has been a trusted name on Newport Street. Our studio is designed to make your tattooing experience as pleasant as possible — whether it's your first piece or your fiftieth.

We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming environment where every client feels at ease. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll experience the passion and professionalism that has earned us our reputation in Bolton and beyond.

What We Do
Our Services
From intricate fine-line work to bold traditional pieces, our artists specialise in bringing your vision to life.
✎

Custom Tattoo Design

Every piece starts with your story. Our artists work closely with you to create a completely bespoke design that's uniquely yours. Consultations are always free.

💎

Professional Piercing

Expert piercing services for men and women. Our trained piercers ensure a safe, comfortable experience with premium-quality jewellery and aftercare guidance.

🎨

All Styles Covered

Traditional, neo-traditional, realism, fine-line, geometric, Japanese, blackwork — whatever your vision, we have an artist who specialises in it.

💬

Free Consultations

Not sure what you want? Come in for a free chat. We'll help you explore ideas, placement, sizing, and style — no pressure, no commitment.

⚡

Cover-Ups & Reworks

Got a tattoo you're not happy with? Our artists are experienced in transforming old ink into something you'll love again.

Walk-In Sessions
