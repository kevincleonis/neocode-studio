A Grooming Experience Your Dog Will Actually Love

At Perfect Paws Grooming & Spa, we believe every dog deserves to be pampered. Based right here on Okehampton Street in Exeter, we've been transforming scruffy pups into show-stoppers for over 8 years.

Our City & Guilds certified groomers take the time to understand each dog's personality and needs. Whether it's a nervous rescue having their first professional groom or a regular who loves the blow-dry, we treat every visit like it's special.

Our Spa Services

From a quick tidy-up to the full luxury spa experience, we've got every pup covered.

Full Groom

The complete package: bath, blow-dry, full clip or hand-strip, ear cleaning, nail trim, and finishing spritz. Your dog will leave looking (and smelling) incredible.

Puppy's First Groom

A gentle introduction to grooming for puppies. We take it slow, building confidence with lots of treats and praise. Perfect for pups from 12 weeks old.

🚿

Bath & Dry

A thorough wash with premium shampoo and conditioner, followed by a professional blow-dry. Ideal between full grooms to keep your dog fresh and clean.

✂

Nail Trimming

Quick, stress-free nail trims by experienced groomers. We'll get those nails to a comfortable length without any fuss. Walk-ins welcome for this service.

🦷

Teeth Cleaning

Keep your pup's smile sparkling with our gentle teeth cleaning service. Using dog-safe products to remove buildup and freshen breath naturally.

🐶

De-shedding Treatment

Say goodbye to tumbleweeds of fur at home. Our specialist de-shedding treatment removes loose undercoat and reduces shedding for weeks.
