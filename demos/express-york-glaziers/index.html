Our Services

Professional Glazing Solutions

From emergency boarding up to precision glass cutting, we deliver expert glazing services across York and the surrounding areas.

Emergency Glazing

Rapid response 24/7 for smashed windows, break-ins, and storm damage. We'll secure your property fast and fit replacement glass the same day wherever possible.

Double Glazing Repair

Misted, cracked, or blown double glazing units replaced quickly. We restore thermal efficiency and eliminate condensation between panes without full frame replacement.

Window Replacement

Complete window installation including uPVC, aluminium, and timber frames. Energy-efficient A-rated glass as standard with all new window fittings.

Glass Cutting

Precision glass cutting to any size and specification. Toughened, laminated, patterned, and obscure glass available for collection or delivery across York.

Shopfront Glazing

Commercial shopfront glass installation, repair, and replacement. Toughened safety glass, automatic doors, and full shopfront fit-outs for retail premises.

Boarding Up & Mirror Fitting

Emergency board-up service to secure your property immediately. Plus bespoke mirror cutting and fitting for bathrooms, gyms, and feature walls.
