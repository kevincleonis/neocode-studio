What We Do

Our Services

From emergency repairs to smart home installations, we handle all aspects of domestic and commercial electrical work.

⚡

Full & Partial Rewiring

Complete rewiring services for older properties. We'll bring your electrics up to current regulations with minimal disruption to your daily routine.

🔍

Electrical Testing & Inspection

EICR certificates, PAT testing, and periodic inspections. Essential for landlords, businesses, and homeowners wanting peace of mind.

🔋

Fuse Board Upgrades

Upgrade your old fuse box to a modern consumer unit with RCD protection. Safer, more efficient, and compliant with Part P regulations.

💡

Lighting Design & Install

Transform your space with professional lighting. LED downlights, garden lighting, feature walls, and complete lighting schemes for any room.

🆘

Emergency Callouts

Power outage? Sparking socket? Tripped fuse board? Don't panic. We offer 24/7 emergency electrical callouts across Reading and Berkshire.

🏠

Smart Home Installation

Smart lighting, automated heating controls, voice-activated systems, and home security. We'll future-proof your property with the latest tech.
