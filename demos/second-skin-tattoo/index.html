How It Works

Our Process

Getting tattooed at Second Skin is a straightforward, enjoyable experience from start to finish.

1

Consultation

We sit down together to talk through your idea, placement, size, and style. No pressure, no rush.

2

Design

Your artist creates a custom design based on our conversation. We'll refine it until it's exactly right.

3

Tattoo

On the day, we walk you through everything. You'll be comfortable and confident the entire session.

4

Aftercare

We send you home with clear aftercare instructions and we're always available if you've got questions.
