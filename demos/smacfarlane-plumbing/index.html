What We Do

Plumbing & Heating Services

From emergency repairs to full installations, we handle every job with the same care and attention to detail.

Boiler Installation

New boiler fitting with a full system clean using the ADEY MagnaCleanse before installation. We make sure your new boiler runs at peak efficiency from day one.

Central Heating

Full central heating installs, repairs, and MagnaCleanse power flushing. We'll get your radiators running hot and your home feeling comfortable again.

Boiler Servicing

Annual boiler servicing to keep your warranty valid and your system running safely. We'll check everything and email you the paperwork straight away.

Bathroom Installation

Complete bathroom refits from start to finish. New sinks, toilets, vanity units, showers, towel rails, and everything in between. Clean, tidy work every time.

Gas Work & Safety

Gas Safe registered for all gas work including cooker connections, gas fire servicing, and landlord gas safety certificates (CP12). Your safety is our priority.

Emergency Repairs
