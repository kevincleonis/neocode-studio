Tattoo Aftercare

Getting the tattoo is only half the job. Here's how to keep it looking fresh.

1

Keep It Wrapped

Leave the wrap on for 2-4 hours after your session. This protects the open skin from bacteria and debris.

2

Wash Gently

Use lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap to gently clean the area. Pat dry with a clean paper towel - never rub.

3

Moisturise Daily

Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser or tattoo balm 2-3 times a day for the first two weeks.

4

Don't Pick or Scratch

It'll peel and itch as it heals. That's normal. Resist the urge to pick at it. Let it heal naturally for the best results.

5

Avoid Sun & Pools

Keep it out of direct sunlight, swimming pools, and saunas for at least 2-3 weeks while it heals.

6

Touch-Up If Needed

If any spots fade during healing, we offer free touch-ups within the first 3 months. Just give us a call.
