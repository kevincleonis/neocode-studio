Aftercare

Looking After
Your New Tattoo

Good aftercare is the difference between a tattoo that heals beautifully and one that doesn't. We take this seriously, and so should you.

Every client gets a printed aftercare sheet to take home. If you've got any concerns during healing, just give us a ring and we'll sort you out.

Keep the initial wrap on for 2-4 hours. When you remove it, wash the tattoo gently with lukewarm water and a fragrance-free antibacterial soap. Pat dry with a clean paper towel. Don't re-wrap it.
We recommend a thin layer of unscented moisturiser like Hustle Butter, coconut oil, or Bepanthen. Apply 2-3 times daily for the first two weeks. Less is more here. You don't want to suffocate the skin.
Quick showers are fine from day one. Just avoid soaking the tattoo. No baths, swimming pools, hot tubs, or saunas for at least 2-3 weeks. The less time under running water, the better during healing.
Totally normal. After about 3-5 days, your tattoo will start to peel and flake like a sunburn. Don't pick at it. Let the skin shed naturally. Pulling off flakes can rip out the ink and cause patchy healing.
Give it at least 48 hours before any heavy exercise. Sweat can irritate a fresh tattoo. If the tattoo is in an area that stretches a lot during workouts (like ribs or inner arm), wait a full week.
Yes. We offer one free touch-up within 3 months of your original session, as long as you've followed the aftercare instructions properly. Just get in touch and we'll get you booked in.
