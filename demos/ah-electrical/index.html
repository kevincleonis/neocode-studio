What We Do

Electrical Services You Can Count On

From a single socket to a full house rewire, we handle it all. Every job is completed to the highest standard and certified with your local council.

Full & Partial Rewires

Complete house rewiring and partial upgrades to bring your property up to current BS 7671 standards. All work notified with building control.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Replace old fuse boxes with modern RCD-protected consumer units. Part P certified and compliant with the latest 18th Edition regulations.

EICR Reports

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for landlords, letting agencies and homeowners. Essential for insurance compliance and safety certification.

Lighting Design & Install

LED downlights, under-cabinet strips, outdoor security lights, automated systems and decorative chandeliers. We bring your vision to life.

EV Charger Installation

Home and commercial EV charging point installations. We handle the full setup from supply upgrade through to final commissioning and certification.

Smart Home & Security

Ring doorbells, CCTV systems, smart lighting, burglar alarms and smoke detectors. Make your home safer and smarter with professional installation.

Electric Showers & Heating

Electric shower replacement, underfloor heating, heated towel rails and immersion heaters. All installations tested and certified for safety.

Commercial Electrician

From retail shops to industrial units, we handle everything from low-load lighting to high-demand 3 phase circuits. Minimal disruption guaranteed.

24/7 Emergency Callouts

Power out? Burning smell? Don't wait. Our emergency service runs round the clock with no callout charge, just a fixed hourly rate. We're there fast.
