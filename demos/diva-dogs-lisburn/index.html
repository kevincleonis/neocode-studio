Lisburn's Favourite Dog Grooming Salon

At Diva Dogs Lisburn, we believe every dog deserves to be pampered. Located at 2B Sloan Street in the heart of Lisburn, we've been turning scruffy pups into show-stoppers for years.

Our team of qualified groomers uses only premium, dog-friendly products and takes the time to understand each dog's unique needs. Whether it's a full groom, a puppy's first trim, or a relaxing bath, your four-legged friend is in safe hands.
