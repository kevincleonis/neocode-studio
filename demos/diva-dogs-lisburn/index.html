Our Grooming Services

From a quick tidy-up to the full works, we've got your pup covered.

✂️

Full Groom

The complete package: bath, blow dry, full haircut, ear cleaning, nail trim and a finishing spritz. Your dog will leave looking and smelling incredible.

🛁

Wash & Dry

A thorough bath using premium shampoos suited to your dog's coat type, followed by a professional blow dry. Perfect between full grooms.

🧴

Hand Stripping

Specialist technique for wire-coated breeds like Terriers and Schnauzers. Keeps the coat healthy, textured and looking its natural best.

💫

Nail Trimming

Quick and careful nail clipping to keep your dog comfortable and prevent overgrowth. Available as a standalone service or add-on.

🐾

Puppy First Groom

A gentle, introductory session for puppies. We take it slow to build positive associations with grooming from an early age. Ideal for pups 12 weeks+.

💨

De-Shedding Treatment

Reduce shedding by up to 80% with our specialist de-shedding bath and blow out. A lifesaver for double-coated breeds and your furniture.
