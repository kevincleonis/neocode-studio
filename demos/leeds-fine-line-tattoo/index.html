Lee Emmerson

Owner & Lead Artist

25+ years behind the needle. Lee founded Fine Line in 2003 and built it into one of Middleton's most trusted studios. His signature style is black and grey portraiture, but he's equally at home with realism, freehand, and intricate cover-ups. Often has a waiting list, which says it all.

What We Do

Styles & Specialities

From photorealistic portraits to delicate fine line work, we cover every style with the same dedication to quality.

Portraits & Realism

Photorealistic portraits, wildlife, and objects captured in stunning detail. Lee Emmerson's signature style, refined over 25 years.

Fine Line & Minimal

Delicate, precise linework for elegant, understated pieces. Single needle and fine gauge work for the cleanest possible lines.

Cover-Ups & Reworks

Got ink you regret? We specialise in transforming old or unwanted tattoos into pieces you'll be proud to show off. Free consultations.

Japanese & Traditional

Full sleeves, back pieces, and traditional Japanese motifs. Bold colour, strong composition, and respect for the craft's heritage.

Tribal & Polynesian

Authentic patterns and custom designs rooted in cultural traditions. Bold, flowing compositions that work with your body's natural lines.

Script & Lettering

From elegant calligraphy to bold typography. Names, quotes, dates, and custom lettering in any style, always crisp and readable.

Walk-Ins Always Welcome

No appointment needed for consultations. Pop in, have a chat about your ideas, and we'll take it from there. No pressure, no hard sell, just honest advice from experienced artists.
