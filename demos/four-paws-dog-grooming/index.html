About Us

More Than a Salon. A Second Home for Your Dog.

Four Paws has been part of the Childwall community since 2001. That's 25 years of wagging tails, happy owners, and dogs that actually enjoy their grooming appointments.

We're not a production line. Every dog gets one-to-one time with their own groomer. No cages, no rushing, no stress. Just patient, qualified professionals who genuinely love what they do.

Nervous or Anxious Dog? You're in the Right Place.

We understand that some dogs find grooming stressful. Our groomers are trained to work with anxious, fearful, and rescue dogs. We'll take all the time they need, use calm handling, and make sure they feel safe throughout. Many of our most loyal customers came to us because their dog had a bad experience elsewhere.
