Meet The Artist

Ben Boston — Born & Inked in Bristol

Born in Bristol in 1976, Ben showed exceptional artistic talent from childhood. After studying art at Brunel and Filton Colleges, he attended his first tattoo conventions in 1996 and never looked back.

By 1998, Ben had taken over the studio on Cheltenham Road, building it into one of Bristol's most trusted tattoo destinations alongside colleague Steuart Kingsley.

  • Award-winning tattoo artist
  • All styles — realism, traditional, Japanese, geometric
  • Specialist in cover-up transformations
  • Relaxed, welcoming studio atmosphere
  • Professional tattooing at painless prices
