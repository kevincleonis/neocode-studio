Welcome to K9 Heaven

A Grooming Experience Your Dog Will Actually Enjoy

We get it - not every dog loves bath time. That's why we've created a space that's calm, gentle, and built around your dog's comfort. No rushed appointments, no stressful assembly lines. Just one-on-one attention from groomers who genuinely love what they do.

Our Services

Grooming Packages for Every Pup

From a quick tidy-up to a full pamper session, we've got your dog covered. Literally.

Full Groom

The complete package: bath, blow dry, full clip or hand strip, nail trim, ear clean, and a spritz of finishing cologne. Your dog leaves looking and smelling amazing.

From £30

Bath & Tidy

A refreshing bath with premium shampoo, blow dry, face and feet trim, nail clip, and ear clean. Perfect between full grooms to keep your dog fresh.

From £20

Puppy's First Groom

A gentle introduction for puppies aged 12 weeks+. We take it slow, build positive associations, and make their first grooming experience stress-free and fun.

From £15

Hand Stripping

Specialist technique for wire-coated breeds like Terriers, Schnauzers and Spaniels. Maintains the natural coat texture and keeps colours vibrant.

From £40

De-shedding Treatment

Heavy shedder? We use specialist tools and shampoos to remove loose undercoat, reduce shedding by up to 80%, and keep your home fur-free.

From £25

Spa Day Package

The ultimate pamper experience. Full groom plus blueberry facial, paw balm treatment, teeth brushing, and a bandana. Because your dog deserves to feel special.

From £45
