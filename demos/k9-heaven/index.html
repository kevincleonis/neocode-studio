About Us

Grooming with Love and Care

K9 Heaven Grooming Salon is a family-run dog grooming business based in the heart of Keady, Co Armagh. We've been helping local dogs look their absolute best for years, building trust with pet owners across the area.

Every dog that walks through our door gets treated like one of our own. We use gentle techniques, quality products, and take the time to make sure your furry friend is comfortable throughout the whole experience.

Our Services

Everything Your Dog Needs

From a quick tidy-up to a full pampering session, we've got all your grooming needs covered under one roof.

✂

Full Groom

The complete package. Includes bath, blow dry, full coat trim, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and a spritz of finishing spray. Your dog will look and smell amazing.

🚿

Bath & Blow Dry

A thorough wash with breed-appropriate shampoo, followed by a professional blow dry. Perfect for keeping your dog fresh between full grooms.

✍

Hand Stripping

Specialist hand stripping for wire-coated breeds like terriers. Maintains coat texture and colour the way nature intended.

💅

Nail Clipping

Quick, careful nail trimming to keep your dog comfortable and prevent overgrown nails. Can be booked as a standalone service.

🦷

Teeth Cleaning

Non-anaesthetic teeth cleaning to reduce tartar build-up and freshen breath. Great for your dog's overall health and wellbeing.

🐶

Puppy Introduction

A gentle first grooming experience designed for puppies. We take it slow so they build positive associations with being groomed from the start.
