What We Do

Professional Cleaning Services

From everyday carpets to delicate upholstery, we deliver outstanding results across every job.

Carpet Cleaning

Deep hot-water extraction cleaning that removes embedded dirt, allergens, and bacteria. Your carpets will look and feel brand new with lasting freshness.

Upholstery Cleaning

Expert cleaning for sofas, armchairs, and all fabric furniture. We use gentle yet powerful methods to restore your upholstery without any damage.

Rug Cleaning

Specialist rug cleaning for all types including wool, silk, and oriental rugs. Careful treatment that preserves colours and fibres beautifully.

Stain Removal

Stubborn stains from wine, coffee, pets, or ink? Our targeted stain removal treatments tackle even the toughest marks for impressive results.

End of Tenancy

Moving out? Get your full deposit back with our thorough end-of-tenancy carpet cleaning. We help tenants and landlords across Staffordshire.

Commercial Cleaning

Keep your business premises spotless with regular commercial carpet cleaning. Flexible scheduling to minimise disruption, maximum impact on appearance.
