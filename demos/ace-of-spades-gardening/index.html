Expert Advice

Seasonal Garden Tips

Keep your garden thriving all year with these quick tips from our team.

🌸

Spring

It's time to prep your beds, sow seeds, and get ahead of the weeds. Feed your lawn early and you'll thank yourself come June.

☀️

Summer

Water deeply in the mornings, not evenings. Deadhead flowers regularly to keep them blooming, and don't cut your grass too short during dry spells.

🍂

Autumn

Clear fallen leaves, plant spring bulbs, and give your lawn its last feed. It's also the perfect time to plant trees and shrubs while the soil's still warm.

❄️

Winter

Protect tender plants from frost, service your tools, and plan next year's garden. Winter's the best time to prune deciduous trees and reshape hedges.
