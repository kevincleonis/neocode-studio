Our Courses

Lessons That Fit Your Life

From complete beginners to experienced drivers wanting extra skills, we've got a course that's right for you.

🌱

Beginner Lessons

Never sat behind the wheel before? No worries. We'll start from scratch and build your skills step by step.

  • Cockpit drill and car controls
  • Moving off, stopping, and steering
  • Junctions, roundabouts, and road positioning
  • Progress at your own speed
⚡

Semi-Intensive Course

Want to pass quicker without the pressure of a full intensive? This is the sweet spot for most learners.

  • 3-4 lessons per week
  • Build confidence faster
  • Test-ready in weeks, not months
  • Ideal for semi-experienced learners
🚀

Intensive Course

Need your licence fast? Our intensive crash course is designed to get you test-ready in the shortest time possible.

  • 30-40 hours over 1-2 weeks
  • Full-day lessons available
  • Test booking assistance included
  • Best for quick turnaround
🔄

Refresher Lessons

Already passed but haven't driven in a while? We'll help you dust off those skills and feel comfortable again.

  • Rebuild confidence behind the wheel
  • Motorway and dual carriageway practice
  • Night driving if needed
  • Book as many sessions as you want
🏆

Pass Plus

You've passed your test. Now take it further. Pass Plus helps new drivers tackle roads and conditions they didn't cover in lessons.

  • Motorway and dual carriageway driving
  • Night, weather, and rural driving
  • Can reduce your insurance premiums
  • DVSA-registered course
