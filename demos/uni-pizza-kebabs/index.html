Our Story

Good food, honest prices, open late

We've been feeding Perry Barr and the wider Birmingham community for over a decade. What started as a small takeaway on Birchfield Road has become a go-to spot for late-night cravings, family dinners, and everything in between.

Every pizza is hand-stretched to order, every kebab is freshly grilled, and we don't cut corners on ingredients. It's simple food done properly, that's our thing.
