About Lock Solid

Meet Bryan. Your Local Glasgow Locksmith.

He's seen it all. From panicked midnight lockouts to full commercial security overhauls, Bryan treats every job with the same level of care and professionalism. There's no call too small and no lock too tricky.

What We Do

Services You Can Count On

From emergency lockouts to high-security installations, we've got you covered. Every job comes with a no-obligation quote and honest pricing.

Urgent

Emergency Lockout

Locked out at 3am? Don't worry. We're available 24/7, 365 days a year. Fast response, non-destructive entry wherever possible, and we won't leave until you're safely inside.

Lock Changes & Upgrades

Moving into a new place? Had a break-in? We'll replace your locks with high-security options from trusted brands. Quick fitting, clean finish, and keys handed straight to you.

UPVC Door & Window Locks

UPVC mechanisms can be tricky, but that's our bread and butter. We repair and replace multipoint locks, handles, cylinders, and letterboxes on all makes and models.

Commercial Security

We work with shops, offices, warehouses, and landlords across Glasgow. Access control, high-security cylinders, restricted key systems, and emergency boarding. All BS3621 compliant.

Master Key Systems

Need one key that opens every door? We design and install master key suites for landlords, property managers, and businesses. Full control, less hassle, maximum security.

Safe Opening & Repair

Lost the combination? Key snapped in the lock? We open safes without damaging the contents. We also supply and fit new safes for homes and businesses.

Why Glasgow Trusts Us

We're not the biggest. But we're the most reliable. Here's what sets us apart from the rest.

30 Min Average Response

When you're locked out, every minute feels like an hour. That's why we aim to be with you within 30 minutes across Glasgow.

No Hidden Charges

We'll give you an honest quote before we start. No call-out fees, no surprise extras, and no inflated "emergency" pricing.

Fully Insured & DBS Checked

Peace of mind comes standard. Bryan and every member of the team is fully insured, DBS checked, and vetted.

Non-Destructive Entry

We always try to get you in without damaging the lock. In most cases, we can pick or bypass the mechanism and save you the cost of a replacement.

BS3621 Compliant Locks

Every lock we fit meets British Standard BS3621, which means it's insurance-approved. No cutting corners, ever.

Local, Not a Call Centre

You'll never be passed around between operators. Call us and you'll speak to Bryan directly. That personal touch is everything.
