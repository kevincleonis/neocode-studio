What We Offer

Our Specialist Services

Comprehensive, evidence-based treatments tailored to your body's needs, delivered by experienced registered osteopaths.

Traditional Osteopathy

Hands-on structural treatment for back pain, neck pain, joint stiffness and postural problems. Gentle manipulation to restore movement and relieve discomfort.

Cranial Osteopathy

A subtle, gentle approach ideal for babies, children, and those with sensitive conditions. Addresses headaches, migraines, and tension patterns through light touch.

Acupuncture & Dry Needling

Western medical acupuncture and trigger point dry needling for chronic pain, muscle tension, and inflammation. Effective for sports injuries and repetitive strain.

Sports Massage

Deep tissue and sports-specific massage therapy to enhance recovery, improve flexibility, and prevent injury. Suitable for athletes and active individuals.

Migraine & Headache Treatment

Specialist assessment and treatment of chronic headaches, migraines, and cervicogenic pain. Combining cranial techniques with targeted manual therapy.

Joint & Arthritis Care

Targeted treatment for osteoarthritis, tennis elbow, golfer's elbow, and degenerative joint conditions. Improving mobility and reducing pain naturally.

About the Clinic

Trusted Osteopathic Care Since 2000

Wellfield Osteopathic Clinic has been providing expert hands-on treatment to patients across South Wales for over two decades. We treat patients of all ages, from newborn babies to the elderly, with a holistic approach that addresses the root cause of pain rather than just the symptoms.

With three clinic locations in Cardiff, Newport, and Penarth, we make quality osteopathic care accessible throughout the region. Our practitioners are members of the British Osteopathic Association and the Institute of Osteopathy.
