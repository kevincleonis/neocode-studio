About Us

Foot Care That Comes to You

Professional chiropody in the comfort and privacy of your own home across Liverpool and the surrounding areas.

At Home Comfort Chiropody Liverpool, we believe everyone deserves access to quality foot care, especially those who find it difficult to travel. Whether it's due to mobility issues, a busy schedule, or simply preferring the comfort of home, we've got you covered.

Our qualified chiropodist brings all the necessary equipment directly to your door. Every visit is carried out with the same standard of care you'd expect from a clinic, just without the waiting room.

We treat patients of all ages across Liverpool and take extra pride in looking after elderly patients and those with underlying health conditions like diabetes.

Our Services

Complete Foot Care, Right at Home

From routine maintenance to specialist treatments, we provide a full range of chiropody services in the comfort of your own home.

💅

Nail Care & Cutting

Professional nail trimming, shaping and care for thick, overgrown or difficult nails. We'll keep your toenails healthy, comfortable and looking their best.

🦶

Callus & Corn Removal

Gentle, effective removal of hard skin, calluses and corns that cause pain or discomfort. We'll also advise you on how to prevent them coming back.

❤️

Diabetic Foot Care

Specialist foot assessments and ongoing care for diabetic patients. We understand the importance of regular checks and take a careful, thorough approach every time.

🩹

Ingrown Toenail Treatment

Safe, careful management and treatment of ingrown toenails to relieve pain and prevent infection. Don't suffer in silence, we can help.

✨

Verruca Treatment

Effective verruca treatment options tailored to your needs. We'll assess each case individually and recommend the best course of action for lasting results.

📋

Biomechanical Assessment

A thorough assessment of your foot mechanics, posture and gait. Ideal if you're experiencing recurring pain or discomfort in your feet, knees or lower back.
