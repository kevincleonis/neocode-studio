Our Services

Comprehensive Foot Care at Home

From routine nail care to specialist diabetic assessments, we bring professional podiatry directly to you.

Diabetic Foot Care

Specialist assessment and ongoing care for diabetic patients, monitoring circulation and nerve function to prevent complications.

Nail Cutting & Care

Professional nail trimming for thickened, ingrown, or difficult nails. Gentle and pain-free treatment in your own home.

Corn & Callus Removal

Expert removal of painful corns and hard skin using sterile instruments. Immediate relief and prevention advice included.

Verruca Treatment

Effective treatment options for stubborn verrucae using the latest clinical methods, tailored to your individual needs.

Biomechanical Assessment

Thorough gait analysis and biomechanical evaluation to identify the root cause of foot, knee, or back pain.

General Foot Care

Routine foot health check-ups, skin care, and maintenance. Perfect for keeping your feet in top condition year-round.

About Us

Experienced Podiatry, Delivered to You

Home Comfort Chiropody was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves access to quality foot care, regardless of mobility. We bring fully equipped, professional podiatry services directly to your home across Liverpool and Merseyside.

Our qualified podiatrist has years of clinical experience treating a wide range of foot conditions. Whether you're elderly, have limited mobility, or simply prefer the convenience of home visits, we ensure you receive the same standard of care as any clinic — in the comfort of your favourite armchair.
