Treatments

Conditions We Treat

From sports injuries to chronic pain, we provide evidence-based physiotherapy tailored to your recovery.

Back & Neck Pain

Thorough assessment and treatment of spinal conditions including sciatica, disc problems, whiplash, and chronic postural pain. Manual therapy combined with exercise rehabilitation.

Sports Injuries

Expert rehabilitation for sports-related injuries. Ligament sprains, muscle tears, tendon injuries, and return-to-sport programmes. Including Pilates-based recovery.

Post-Surgery Rehab

Specialist pre and post-operative management for joint replacements, ACL reconstructions, and shoulder surgery. Structured programmes to get you back to full function.

Shoulder & Upper Limb

Clare Longton's specialist area. Frozen shoulder, rotator cuff, post-oncology rehabilitation, TMJ dysfunction, and complex upper quadrant conditions.

Acupuncture

Medical acupuncture by AACP accredited practitioner Mark Ghee. Evidence-based pain relief for musculoskeletal conditions, headaches, and chronic pain management.

Massage & Manual Therapy

Therapeutic and sports massage, spinal manipulation, joint mobilisation, and soft tissue techniques. Hands-on treatment combined with exercise prescription.
