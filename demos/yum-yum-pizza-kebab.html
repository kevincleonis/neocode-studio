About Us

Birmingham's Go-To Takeaway on Coventry Road

At Yum Yum Pizza & Kebab, we believe great food starts with great ingredients. Our dough is made fresh every morning, our kebab meats are marinated for 24 hours, and every dish leaves our kitchen with pride.

Located on Coventry Road in the heart of Sheldon, we've been serving the local community with delicious pizzas, kebabs, burgers, and fish & chips. Whether you're picking up on the way home or ordering delivery for a family night in, we guarantee flavour that keeps you coming back.
