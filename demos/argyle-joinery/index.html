What We Do

Expert Joinery Services

From traditional joinery to modern installations, we've got the skills and experience to handle it all. Here's what we can do for you.

Fitted Kitchens

Complete kitchen design and installation, from bespoke cabinetry to worktop fitting. We'll transform your kitchen into something you'll actually enjoy spending time in.

Doors & Door Hanging

Internal and external doors, supplied and fitted to perfection. We handle everything from standard replacements to custom-made doors that are built to last.

Staircases

Staircase construction, renovation, and repair. Whether you want a classic timber staircase or a modern open-tread design, we'll build it right.

Flooring

Hardwood, engineered, and laminate flooring, installed with precision. We'll make sure every plank sits perfectly, with clean finishes and no squeaky boards.

Bespoke Furniture

Custom-built shelving, wardrobes, storage solutions, and one-of-a-kind furniture pieces. If you can imagine it, chances are we can build it.

Renovations

Full property renovations and refurbishments. We'll breathe new life into tired spaces, handling all the joinery and carpentry work your project needs.
