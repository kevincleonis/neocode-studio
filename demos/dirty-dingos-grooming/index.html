Our Services

Grooming Tailored to Your Dog

From a full pamper session to a quick nail clip, we offer everything your pup needs to look and feel their best.

Full Groom

Complete wash, dry, clip, and style. The full works for a fresh, happy dog from nose to tail.

Bath & Brush

A thorough wash and brush-out to keep your dog's coat clean, shiny, and free of tangles.

Puppy First Groom

A gentle, positive introduction to grooming for puppies. Building trust and confidence from day one.

Hand Stripping

Expert hand stripping for wire-coated breeds. Maintains natural coat texture and healthy skin.

Nail Clipping & De-matting

Quick nail trims and careful de-matting to keep your dog comfortable and mat-free.

Breed-Specific & Teeth Cleaning

Tailored breed cuts and dental care to keep your dog looking show-ready with a healthy smile.
