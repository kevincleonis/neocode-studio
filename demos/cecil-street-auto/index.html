What We Do

From routine check-ups to the tricky stuff, we've got you covered. Here's what we can help with.

🛠

MOT Testing

We're a fully authorised MOT testing centre. Book your annual test with us and we'll get you sorted quickly, with clear advice on any advisories.

⚙

Servicing

Interim and full services to keep your car in top shape. We follow manufacturer schedules and use quality parts to protect your warranty.

🔧

Repairs

Brakes, clutches, exhausts, suspension... if it's broken, we'll fix it. We handle everything from minor repairs to major engine work.

💻

Diagnostics

Warning light on? Our advanced diagnostic equipment will pinpoint the issue fast, saving you time and money on unnecessary parts.

❄

Air Conditioning

Full air con regas and leak testing. Don't sweat it this summer. We'll get your system blowing cold again in no time.

⚪

Tyres

We supply and fit tyres from all the major brands at competitive prices. Balancing, alignment and puncture repairs all available.
