What We Do

Professional Locksmith Services

From emergency lockouts to full security upgrades, we provide expert locksmith solutions across Leicester and the surrounding areas.

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your home, office or car? We provide fast, non-destructive entry 24 hours a day with no call-out charge.

Lock Changes

Moving home or need enhanced security? We supply and fit British Standard locks from leading manufacturers at competitive prices.

Lock Repairs

Stiff, jammed or faulty locks repaired quickly by our expert engineers. We fix all types of domestic and commercial locks.
