What We Do

Our Plumbing Services

From quick fixes to major installations, we've got every plumbing need covered across Stirling and Central Scotland.

Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipes, flooding, or a major leak? We're available 24/7, 365 days a year. We'll be at your door fast, usually within the hour.

Boiler Servicing

Annual boiler servicing, repairs, and full installations. We work with all major brands and can usually source parts same-day.

Bathroom Fitting

Complete bathroom design and installation. From budget-friendly refreshes to luxury wet rooms, we handle every detail.

Central Heating

Full central heating installation, powerflushing, and radiator replacements. We'll keep your home toasty through the Scottish winters.

Drainage & Unblocking

Blocked drains, CCTV drain surveys, and complete drainage solutions. We'll get everything flowing again in no time.

Gas Safety Checks

Landlord gas safety certificates, gas appliance installations, and compliance checks. We're fully Gas Safe registered (you can check us).
