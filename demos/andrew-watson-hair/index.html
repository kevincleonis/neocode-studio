Welcome to Andrew Watson Hairdressing

Located on Belfast's iconic Lisburn Road, Andrew Watson Hairdressing has been helping clients look and feel their best for years. We're not just a salon - we're a destination for anyone who takes their hair seriously.

Our team of skilled stylists combines classic technique with modern trends, delivering results that turn heads. Whether you're after a fresh cut, a bold new colour, or the perfect bridal look, we've got you covered.

We believe great hair starts with a genuine consultation. Every client gets our full attention, because your style should be as individual as you are.

Our Services

From precision cuts to transformative colour, we offer a full range of services to keep you looking your absolute best.

✂

Cuts & Styling

Precision cuts tailored to your face shape, hair type and lifestyle. From classic bobs to contemporary textured styles, we'll find the perfect look for you.

◈

Colour Services

Full colour, highlights, balayage, ombre and colour correction. Our colourists use top-tier products to deliver vibrant, long-lasting results every time.

✿

Blow Dry & Finish

Whether it's a smooth, sleek blow dry or bouncy curls for a night out, our stylists will have you leaving the salon feeling like a million pounds.

❅

Bridal Hair

Your wedding day deserves perfect hair. We offer full bridal packages including trials, on-the-day styling, and bridesmaids' hair. Let's make it unforgettable.

★

Hair Treatments

Deep conditioning, keratin smoothing, Olaplex repair and scalp treatments. Give your hair the TLC it deserves with our restorative treatment menu.

⚦

Men's Grooming

Sharp cuts, fades, beard trims and classic gentleman's styling. Our team knows how to deliver a look that's clean, confident and effortlessly sharp.
