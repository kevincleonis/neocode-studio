Our Story

More Than Just a Haircut

At Andrew Watson Hairdressing, we believe that a great hairstyle can transform how you feel. Nestled on the iconic Lisburn Road in Belfast, our salon has been a destination for those who value exceptional craftsmanship and a genuinely warm experience.

Our team stays ahead of the curve with ongoing training in the latest techniques, premium products, and a keen eye for what suits you best. Whether it's a fresh colour, a precision cut, or bridal styling for your big day, we're here to make you look and feel incredible.
