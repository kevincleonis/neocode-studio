What We Do

Locksmith Services You Can Count On

From emergency lockouts to full security overhauls, we handle it all. Every job comes with a guarantee and zero call-out fees.

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your home, car, or office? We'll be there in 30 minutes or less, 24 hours a day. No call-out charge, ever.

Lock Changes

Moved into a new property? Had a break-in? We'll swap out your old locks for high-security replacements in no time.

UPVC Door & Window Locks

UPVC mechanisms can be tricky, but not for us. We repair and replace multipoint locks, handles, and cylinders on all UPVC doors and windows.

Commercial Security

Shops, offices, warehouses. We install and maintain commercial-grade locks, access control systems, and master key suites.

Key Cutting

Need spare keys? We cut standard, dimple, and high-security keys on-site. Bring the original and we'll have yours ready in minutes.

Security Upgrades

Don't wait for a break-in. We'll assess your home or business and recommend upgrades that actually make a difference. Anti-snap, anti-pick, anti-bump.
