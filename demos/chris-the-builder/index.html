Meet Chris

A Builder Who Actually
Loves What He Does

"I love my job" isn't something you hear from many builders. But Chris Smith has been saying it for over 36 years, and the results speak for themselves. Based in Orrell, Wigan, Chris personally oversees every single project from start to finish.

With one time-served employee by his side, Chris doesn't send strangers to your home. You get the man himself, on site, every day, until the job is done right. He won't start a new major project until yours is complete.

That dedication earned him the LABC Construction Specialist of the Year award and five consecutive years of Wigan Good Trader Scheme excellence. It's also why he's never had a single complaint in over three decades.
