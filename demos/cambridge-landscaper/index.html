Seasonal Garden Tips

Care for Your Garden Year Round

A beautiful garden needs attention throughout the seasons. Here are our top tips for each time of year.

🌸

Spring

Start feeding your lawn, prune roses, divide perennials, and plant summer-flowering bulbs. It's the best time for a garden overhaul.

☀️

Summer

Water deeply in the evening, deadhead flowers regularly, mow weekly, and keep on top of weeds while the ground is soft.

🍂

Autumn

Plant spring bulbs, collect fallen leaves for compost, aerate your lawn, and it's the ideal time for new patio or fencing projects.

❄️

Winter

Protect tender plants with fleece, plan next year's garden design, clean and oil tools, and repair any damaged fencing or paths.
