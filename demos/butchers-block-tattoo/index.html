About Us

More Than Just a Tattoo Shop

We're a proper custom tattoo parlour in the heart of Wigan. Our team of 9 artists covers every style you could want, and we pride ourselves on making every single customer feel at home from the moment they walk through the door.

Whether it's your first tattoo or your fiftieth, we'll work with you to create something truly personal. Give us an idea and we'll make it better than you ever expected. That's not marketing, that's what our clients actually say.

What We Offer

Services

Everything you need under one roof. Custom tattoos, professional piercings, and more.

Custom Tattoos

Every piece is designed from scratch with you. Bring us your idea, a reference, or just a vague concept and we'll create something completely unique to you.

Cover-Ups & Reworks

Got a tattoo you're not happy with? Our artists specialise in turning old or faded work into something you'll actually love showing off.

Body Piercing

Professional piercing available Thursday through Sunday with Chris. Most piercing types covered including dermals. Sterile, safe, and done right.

Gift Vouchers

Not sure what to get someone? Our gift vouchers are available for any amount and we can post them out to you. Perfect for birthdays and Christmas.

Walk-Ins Welcome

No appointment? No problem. Pop in and see who's available. We always try to fit walk-ins in, especially for smaller pieces and flash designs.

Consultations

Come in for a free chat about your idea. We'll talk through sizing, placement, style, and pricing before you commit to anything. Zero pressure.
