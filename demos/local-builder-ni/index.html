Belfast's Most Trusted Building Contractor

We're a family-run building company based in the Belfast area, and we've been transforming homes across Northern Ireland for over 15 years. No job is too big or too small - from a simple plastering repair to a full house extension, we treat every project like it's our own home.

Our Services

Whatever your building project needs, we've got the skills and experience to deliver it to the highest standard.

House Extensions

Single and double storey extensions to give your family the extra space you need. Designed and built from the ground up.

Full Renovations

Complete home renovations that breathe new life into tired properties. We manage everything from demolition to finishing touches.

🍲

Kitchen Fitting

Bespoke kitchen installations including plumbing, electrics, tiling, and worktops. Your dream kitchen, professionally fitted.

🛀

Bathrooms

Full bathroom design and installation. From wet rooms to luxury suites, we handle all plumbing and tiling in-house.

🏘

Roofing

New roofs, re-roofing, repairs, and flat roof conversions. We keep your home watertight and looking great from the top down.

Plastering

🛣

Driveways & Patios

Block paving, tarmac, and concrete driveways plus stunning patio areas. Transform your outdoor space with lasting quality.

🏢

Commercial Work

Shop fit-outs, office refurbishments, and commercial property maintenance. Professional results on time and on budget.
