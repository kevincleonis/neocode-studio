What We Do

Comprehensive building services for homes and businesses across Northern Ireland

🏢

Extensions

Single and double storey extensions to give your home the extra space it needs. Designed and built to your exact specifications.

🔨

Renovations

Full house renovations and refurbishments. We'll transform tired properties into beautiful, modern living spaces.

🍳

Kitchens

Complete kitchen installations from design to finish, including plumbing, electrics, tiling and fitted units.

🛁

Bathrooms

Full bathroom refits and wet room installations. Quality fixtures and fittings with expert plumbing throughout.

🏠

Roofing

Roof repairs, replacements and new roof installations. Slate, tile and flat roofing work carried out to the highest standard.

🧱

Plastering

Internal and external plastering, skimming, rendering and K-rend finishes for a flawless result every time.

🛣

Driveways

Block paving, tarmac, gravel and concrete driveways. We also handle patios, paths and all groundwork.

🏛

Commercial

Shop fit-outs, office refurbishments and commercial builds. Reliable service with minimal disruption to your business.
