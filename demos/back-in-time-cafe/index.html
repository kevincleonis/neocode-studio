Our Story

Born from a Leap of Faith

Est. July 2018

Two Friends. One Dream. A Whole Lot of Coffee.

When Petra and Mishelle left their council education jobs due to budget cuts, they didn't see an ending. They saw a beginning. After months of planning and plenty of sleepless nights, Back in Time Cafe opened its doors on St Sepulchre Gate in July 2018.

The idea was simple: create a space where people could slow down, enjoy really good coffee from a local UK roaster, and eat honest homemade food without paying through the nose. No gimmicks, no pretension, just proper hospitality.

They survived COVID lockdowns, came back stronger, grew their team, and even started serving cocktails on Friday and Saturday evenings. Seven years later, the cafe is still going strong with a loyal community of regulars who feel more like family.
