What We Do

Our Services

From a single room refresh to a full commercial transformation, we deliver flawless results every time.

Interior Painting

Expert interior painting with premium paints and meticulous preparation. We transform living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens and hallways with a flawless finish.

Exterior Painting

Weather-resistant exterior painting that protects and enhances your property. We use high-quality masonry and wood paints built to last in North East conditions.

Wallpapering

Precise wallpaper hanging for feature walls and full rooms. From classic patterns to modern designs, we achieve seamless joins and perfect pattern matching.

Commercial Decorating

Professional decorating for offices, shops, restaurants and public spaces. Minimal disruption to your business with flexible scheduling including weekends.

Plastering

Smooth, even plaster finishes for walls and ceilings. We repair damaged plaster, skim coat over artex, and prepare surfaces to the highest standard.

Spray Painting

Advanced airless spray painting for smooth, factory-quality finishes on doors, kitchens, uPVC and large surface areas with fast, even coverage.
