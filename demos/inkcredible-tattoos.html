Our Services

Every piece we create is unique. Whether it's your first tattoo or your fiftieth, we deliver precision and artistry you can wear with pride.

01

Custom Tattoos

Fully bespoke designs created in collaboration with you. From concept sketches to the finished piece — your story, inked your way.

02

Cover-Up Work

Expert cover-up tattoos that transform unwanted ink into stunning new artwork. We specialise in creative solutions for any existing piece.

03

Body Piercing

Professional piercing services using premium titanium jewellery. Ears, nose, and body piercings in a sterile, comfortable environment.

04

Tattoo Removal

Safe and effective tattoo removal services. Whether partial or full removal, we use modern techniques for the best possible results.

05

Fine Line & Detail

Delicate, intricate designs with exceptional precision. Perfect for minimalist tattoos, script work, and detailed portraiture.

06

Semi-Permanent Makeup

Enhance your natural beauty with our licensed semi-permanent makeup services. Brows, lips, and liner by trained specialists.

Our Story

Family-Run. Passion-Driven.

Inkcredible Tattoos has been a cornerstone of Oldham's creative scene for over two decades. Our ground-floor studio on Yorkshire Street is fully accessible, council-approved, and equipped with the latest industry-standard equipment. We take pride in creating a welcoming, sterile environment where your comfort is our priority.

Council Approved

Licensed and approved by Oldham Council for your safety and peace of mind.

Fully Accessible

Ground floor studio with full wheelchair access. Everyone is welcome.

Sterile & Safe

Hospital-grade sterilisation and single-use equipment as standard.

Walk-Ins Welcome

Appointments preferred, but walk-ins are always welcome when we have availability.
