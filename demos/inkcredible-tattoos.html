Meet the Team

THE ARTIST

CHRIS MARTIN

Owner & Lead Artist • 20+ Years

Chris has been tattooing for over two decades, building Inkcredible into one of Oldham's most trusted studios. What started as a passion has grown into a family-run business known for quality work and a welcoming atmosphere.

He's built a loyal client base who keep coming back, some since 2011. Whether it's a small first tattoo or a full sleeve, Chris treats every piece with the same level of care and attention to detail.

His cover-up work is particularly popular, turning old tattoos people regret into pieces they're genuinely proud of.
