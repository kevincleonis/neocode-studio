What We Do

Fencing Services

From classic overlap panels to modern slatted designs, we supply and install the full range of premium wooden fencing for every property.

Overlap Fencing

Traditional overlapping panels providing a classic look with excellent coverage and wind resistance for any garden boundary.

Featheredge Fencing

Individually nailed featheredge boards creating a strong, durable fence that can follow sloping ground beautifully.

Closeboard Fencing

The most robust fencing style, built on-site with vertical boards fixed to horizontal rails for maximum strength and privacy.

Picket Fencing

Charming pointed-top picket fences perfect for front gardens, borders, and adding traditional kerb appeal to any property.

Woven & Slatted Fencing

Contemporary woven and slatted panel designs offering a modern aesthetic with excellent airflow and stylish appearance.

Gates & Trellis

Bespoke garden gates and decorative trellis panels to complement your fencing and complete the look of your outdoor space.
