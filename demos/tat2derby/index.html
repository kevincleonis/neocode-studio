Look After Your Ink

Aftercare Tips

A great tattoo is only half the job. Following proper aftercare keeps your piece looking sharp for years.

1

Leave The Wrap On

Keep the initial wrap on for 2-4 hours. It protects the fresh tattoo from bacteria and friction while the skin starts to settle.

2

Wash Gently

Use lukewarm water and a fragrance-free antibacterial soap. Pat dry with a clean paper towel. No rubbing, no cloth towels.

3

Moisturise Daily

Apply a thin layer of recommended tattoo balm or unscented moisturiser 2-3 times daily. Don't smother it, a little goes a long way.

4

Don't Pick or Scratch

Your tattoo will peel and itch as it heals. That's normal. Picking at scabs can pull ink out and leave patchy spots. Resist the urge.

5

Avoid Sun & Water

No swimming pools, hot tubs, or sunbathing for at least 2 weeks. UV exposure fades ink fast, so use SPF 50 once healed.

6

Wear Loose Clothing

Tight clothing can irritate the tattooed area and stick to the healing skin. Loose, breathable fabrics are your best friend during healing.
