What We Do

Complete Vehicle Services
Under One Roof

From routine MOTs to complex diagnostics, our NVQ-qualified team handles it all with genuine and OEM-quality parts.

MOT Testing

Official MOT testing for cars and light commercial vehicles. Fast turnaround with clear pass/fail reporting and honest advice on any advisories.

Full Servicing

Interim and full vehicle servicing to manufacturer specifications. We keep your logbook stamped and your warranty intact.

Diagnostics

Advanced fault-finding using the latest diagnostic equipment. We pinpoint issues with engine management, ABS, airbags and all electronic systems.

Brakes & Clutches

Complete brake and clutch overhauls including disc, pad and caliper replacement. Keeping you safe on the road is our top priority.

Exhausts & Emissions

Full exhaust system repairs, DPF cleaning, and catalytic converter work. We ensure your vehicle meets emissions standards.

Auto Electrics

Starter motor, alternator, and full electrical system diagnostics and repairs. We trace and fix faults other garages miss.

Mobile Repairs

Can't get to us? We'll come to you. Alex started as a mobile mechanic and we still offer callout services across the Telford area.

Suspension & Steering

Shock absorbers, springs, drop links, track rods and wheel alignment. We restore your ride comfort and handling precision.

Commercial & HGV

Not just cars — we service buses, HGVs, commercial vehicles, vintage cars and even boats. If it has an engine, we can fix it.

Our Story

From a Van to
Telford's Trusted Garage

A.J.S Motorworks started with Alex and a van — a mobile mechanic with a passion for honest work. Today, that same dedication drives a full team of highly skilled technicians operating out of Spring Hill Service Station in Wellington.

Every member of the team holds NVQ Level 3 or higher qualifications in Light Vehicle Maintenance & Repair, with continuous training to keep pace with modern vehicle technology. We use only genuine or high-quality OEM replacement parts.
